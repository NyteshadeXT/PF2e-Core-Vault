As they toil, a robed figure supervises them—this is Talabir Welik, the tournament's referee. A neutral wizard and member of the [[Arcane Conclave]] Talabir is unaware of [[5-World/NPCs/Ardentia City/Loris Raknian|Loris Raknian]] evildoing. Talabir is concerned primarily with making sure the games themselves rune smoothly. He takes his responsibility as referee of the Champion's Games very seriously, and follows the rules literally. Welik’s toad familiar, Dreemik spends most of the time dozing in the wizard's coin pouch, pretending to be a dragon sleeping on his hoard.
