- **Role:** Young devotee of [[Sylvan]] and herbal attendant
- **Brief Description:** A quiet elf with moss-colored robes, bark-like tattoos along their arms, and an unsettling tendency to appear silently beside visitors. Syllen often tends living vines and medicinal plants growing throughout the plaza.
- **Personality:** Calm, introspective, intensely attentive, and deeply connected to the surrounding wilderness. Syllen speaks carefully and rarely wastes words.
- **Ancestry:** Elf
- **Heritage:** Woodland Elf
- **Potential Adventure Hook:** Syllen believes portions of the Prismatic Canopy are becoming “sick.” Plants brought from deeper regions sometimes continue growing after being cut, twisting unnaturally toward crystal sources or living creatures.
