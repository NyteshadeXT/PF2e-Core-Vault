- **Role:** Senior caretaker of Lanternrest Shrine Plaza
- **Brief Description:** A middle-aged woman with silver-threaded dark hair, soft features weathered by stress, and layered robes bearing symbols from several faiths rather than a single deity. She moves quietly through the plaza tending candles, speaking with mourners, and checking the Vigil Flame personally each evening.
- **Personality:** Compassionate, patient, quietly exhausted, and deeply empathetic. Elowen believes faith exists to steady people during hardship rather than judge them.
- **Ancestry:** Human
- **Heritage:** Versatile Heritage
- **Potential Adventure Hook:** Elowen has noticed the Vigil Flame briefly changing color during violent storms or when certain travelers enter the plaza. She fears the crystal embers are reacting to something dangerous growing within the Prismatic Canopy.
