- **Role:** Owner and quartermaster of Trailrest Supply and Stable
- **Brief Description:** A broad-shouldered woman in her late forties with weathered skin, practical clothing, and permanently ink-stained fingers from years of maintaining supply ledgers. Maren moves with the confidence of someone who has survived the frontier through preparation rather than luck.
- **Personality:** Blunt, observant, deeply practical, and quietly protective of Springrun’s people. She has little patience for arrogance or careless adventurers but respects competence immediately.
- **Ancestry:** Human
- **Heritage:** Versatile Heritage
- **Potential Adventure Hook:** Maren becomes concerned when several experienced expeditions fail to return from the same trade route within the Prismatic Canopy. The surviving pack animals returned alone, terrified and carrying damaged supply crates covered in strange crystal growths.
