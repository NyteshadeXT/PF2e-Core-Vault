- **Ancestry:** Elf (Ancient)
    
- **Heritage:** Woodland Elf
    
- **Personality:** Calm, clinical, and intensely observant. Valerius has lived long enough to see the world before the Shattering and views the current "distortions" with a scientist's curiosity rather than fear.
    
- **Hook:** Valerius is looking for a "Cursed Bloom" rumored to grow near the Haunted Bridge and will reward the party with _Alchemist's Fire_ if they can retrieve a sample without being "changed" by its spores.
