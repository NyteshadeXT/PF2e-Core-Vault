- **Role:** Groundskeeper and caretaker of the Vigil Flame
- **Brief Description:** An elderly halfling with soot-darkened hands, bushy eyebrows, and an endless collection of tiny tools hanging from his belt. Fenrick spends most of his days cleaning lanterns, replacing prayer ribbons, and carefully maintaining the flame basin.
- **Personality:** Gentle, absent-minded, stubbornly meticulous, and surprisingly observant. Fenrick notices details most others ignore.
- **Ancestry:** Halfling
- **Heritage:** Hillock Halfling
- **Potential Adventure Hook:** Fenrick recently discovered old prayer ribbons scorched from the inside out despite no sign of ordinary fire. Several bore names belonging to missing settlers.
