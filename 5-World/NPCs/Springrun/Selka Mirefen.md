- **Role:** Crystal salvager and field surveyor
- **Brief Description:** A lean goblin woman with layered goggles, patched waterproof gear, and dozens of carefully labeled crystal samples hanging from padded satchels. Selka spends most of her time outside Springrun recovering damaged or unstable crystal formations.
- **Personality:** Bold, irreverent, highly practical, and fascinated by dangerous phenomena most sensible people avoid. Selka treats unstable crystal fields like puzzles waiting to be solved.
- **Ancestry:** Goblin
- **Heritage:** Charhide Goblin
- **Potential Adventure Hook:** Selka recently discovered a partially buried crystal formation deep within the canopy that appears to pulse in response to nearby living creatures. She wants escorts for a second expedition before prospectors or scavengers interfere with the site.
