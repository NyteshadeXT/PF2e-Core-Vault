- **Role:** Master crystalwright and operator of the Resonant Spire
- **Brief Description:** A sharp-featured woman with pale copper skin, tired amber eyes, and protective gloves permanently dusted with crystal residue. Talandra dresses practically, favoring reinforced work coats layered with measuring charms, insulated straps, and tool harnesses.
- **Personality:** Intelligent, cautious, relentlessly methodical, and quietly intense. Talandra trusts observation and procedure over instinct, though recent events are beginning to shake her confidence.
- **Ancestry:** Human
- **Heritage:** Skilled Heritage
- **Potential Adventure Hook:** Talandra has discovered several crystal samples resonating at identical frequencies despite originating from distant locations within the Prismatic Canopy. She fears the phenomenon may indicate a growing network of connected instability beneath the region.
