- **Role:** Wagonwright and repair hand
- **Brief Description:** A lanky man with patched gloves, a crooked nose, and old scars from a wagon collapse years ago. Torren spends most of his time repairing wheels, reinforcing axles, and muttering about “foolish loads and worse roads.”
- **Personality:** Cynical, dry-witted, dependable, and secretly sentimental beneath layers of complaining. He distrusts crystal technology and prefers traditional craftsmanship whenever possible.
- **Ancestry:** Human
- **Heritage:** Skilled Heritage
- **Potential Adventure Hook:** Torren recently recovered a shattered wagon from the forest whose wheel rims had partially fused into smooth glass-like material despite no signs of fire or heat nearby.
