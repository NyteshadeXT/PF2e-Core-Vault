- **Role:** Kitchen Porter and Heavy Lifter.
    
- **Description:** A rudimentary clockwork construct powered by cracked Primal Crystal fragments found in the Ember Bank.
    
- **Personality:** It has no speech but hums with a low melodic frequency that changes pitch based on how much "mana" is in the room.
    
- **Hook:** If the players bring a **Creature Core** near Shards, the construct might temporarily malfunction or "level up" its efficiency, serving as the players' first hint that Dungeon-tech can interface with their village's current technology.
