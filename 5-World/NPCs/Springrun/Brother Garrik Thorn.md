- **Role:** Warden-priest of [[Aegis]] and keeper of Springrun’s protective wards
- **Brief Description:** A broad-shouldered dwarf with a shaved head, iron-gray beard, and numerous repaired scars across his arms. Garrik wears practical armor beneath his layered shrine vestments and carries a heavy lantern-staff engraved with protective sigils.
- **Personality:** Vigilant, disciplined, skeptical, and quietly protective. Garrik trusts preparation and strong defenses more than optimism.
- **Ancestry:** Dwarf
- **Heritage:** Strong-Blooded Dwarf
- **Potential Adventure Hook:** Several warding stones near Springrun’s outer roads have begun failing without obvious cause. Garrik believes something is intentionally interfering with the settlement’s protections from within the forest.
