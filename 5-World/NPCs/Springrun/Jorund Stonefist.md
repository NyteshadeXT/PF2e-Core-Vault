- **Ancestry:** Dwarf (Mountain)
    
- **Personality:** A gruff perfectionist who views smithing as a science. He is the one who pioneered the induction furnace and is fiercely protective of his "secret" crystal-tuning methods.
    
- **Hook:** Jorund is frustrated by the "Dungeon-trait" gear the players will eventually find, as it defies his understanding of metallurgy. He will pay the party to let him "study" (and potentially break) any strange metal items they find.
