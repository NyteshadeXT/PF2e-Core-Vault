- **Role:** Traveling priestess of [[Liriel]]
- **Brief Description:** A cheerful half-elf wanderer with sun-faded cloaks, travel charms woven into her hair, and boots so worn they seem held together entirely by luck and stitching. Alyra rarely stays in one place for long.
- **Personality:** Warm, adventurous, clever, and unpredictably insightful. She treats every stranger like a potential story waiting to happen.
- **Ancestry:** Half-Elf
- **Heritage:** Seer Elf Heritage
- **Potential Adventure Hook:** Alyra recently returned from a distant trade road claiming she encountered travelers who had no reflections while passing through heavy canopy fog. She is trying to determine whether the experience was illusion, curse, or something worse.
