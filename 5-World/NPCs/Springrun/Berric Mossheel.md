- **Role:** Stablemaster and animal handler at Trailrest
- **Brief Description:** A compact, heavily bearded dwarf with moss-green eyes, thick leather gloves, and a calm voice capable of soothing even aggressive beasts. Berric smells faintly of hay, damp earth, and pine oil nearly all the time.
- **Personality:** Quiet, patient, superstitious, and more comfortable around animals than crowds. He notices small behavioral changes others overlook and trusts instincts over theory.
- **Ancestry:** Dwarf
- **Heritage:** Rock Dwarf
- **Potential Adventure Hook:** Several animals under Berric’s care have begun refusing to approach a particular forest trail after sunset. One injured pack lizard returned with strange burns that faintly glow during rainfall.
