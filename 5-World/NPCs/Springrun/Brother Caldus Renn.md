- **Role:** Visiting devotee of [[Arcarios]] and resonance scholar
- **Brief Description:** A quiet older elf with silver robes trimmed in geometric sigils and thin crystal lenses mounted over one eye. Caldus arrived in Springrun claiming to study long-term resonance stability in frontier settlements.
- **Personality:** Reserved, analytical, polite, and difficult to read. Caldus rarely speaks without purpose and watches crystal activity with unsettling focus.
- **Ancestry:** Elf
- **Heritage:** Ancient Elf
- **Potential Adventure Hook:** Caldus privately believes the resonance disturbances near Springrun resemble records tied to early Dungeon manifestations. He seeks discreet assistance investigating whether something beneath the canopy is beginning to awaken.
