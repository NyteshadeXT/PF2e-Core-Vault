- **Role:** Master Leatherworker and Resin-Tanner.
    
- **Description:** A stout, broad-shouldered dwarf with a beard braided with small leather thongs and a permanent scent of pine resin clinging to him. His forearms are covered in faint chemical burns from years of working in the **Resin Vats**.
    
- **Personality:** Bram is a "no-nonsense" survivalist who views the **Prismatic Canopy** as an adversary that must be respected, not a resource to be exploited. He is skeptical of the "Dungeon-seekers" and often grumbles about "soft-handed city folk" underestimating the forest's hostility.
    
- **Hook:** Bram is desperate for "untainted" resin. He believes the local flora is becoming too mutated to produce quality waterproofing sap and will offer a significant discount on gear to any party that can secure a source of pure resin from deeper within the **Prismatic Canopy**
