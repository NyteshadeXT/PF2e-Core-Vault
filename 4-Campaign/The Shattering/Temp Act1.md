# Recommended Encounter Density
You do _not_ want every hex keyed. A good target for this style of exploration campaign:

|Region|Hexes|Keyed Encounters|
|---|--:|--:|
|Prismatic Canopy|~240|16–24|
|Deep Wilds|~120|12–18|

Total:

- roughly **28–42 keyed locations**
    
- plus wandering encounters/random tables.
 
The Deep Wilds should have:
- **higher encounter density**
    
- but more importantly:
- **higher narrative density**

The players should gradually realize:
> “Something is concentrating here.”

---

# Core Design Difference Between Regions

## The Prismatic Canopy

### Tone
- Dangerous frontier wilderness
- Crystal-touched ecosystem
- Mostly natural (or _appearing_ natural)
- Strange but survivable
- Exploration-oriented

### Primary Encounter Themes
- Wildlife
- Environmental hazards
- Resource discoveries
- Territorial creatures
- Faction activity
- Signs something is changing

The Canopy should feel:
> beautiful, dangerous, alive.

---

## The Deep Wilds
### Tone
- Oppressive
- Quiet
- Increasingly unnatural
- Spatially uncertain
- Reality beginning to bend
- The forest becoming influenced by Dungeon resonance

### Primary Encounter Themes
- Mutated creatures
- Dungeon bleed-through
- Resonance phenomena
- Missing expeditions
- Impossible environmental behavior
- Psychological unease
- Proto-dungeon manifestations

The Deep Wilds should feel:
> like the world is beginning to fail.

---

# Random Encounter Tables
A good exploration table should contain:
- combat,
- signs,
- discoveries,
- hazards,
- weather,
- tracks,
- environmental shifts,
- faction activity,
- and foreshadowing.

---

# Keyed Encounter Philosophy

Your keyed encounters should do one of four things:

|Purpose|Example|
|---|---|
|Teach the region|Crystal wildlife|
|Foreshadow the Dungeon|Resonant pulses|
|Build tension|Missing caravans|
|Reveal lore|Archway fragments|

---

# Encounter Density Recommendation

I would personally aim for:

## Prismatic Canopy

### ~20 keyed locations

Broken down roughly into:

- 6 combat
    
- 5 discoveries
    
- 4 environmental
    
- 3 social
    
- 2 weird phenomena
    

---

## Deep Wilds

### ~15 keyed locations

Broken down roughly into:

- 5 dangerous combat
    
- 4 resonance/weirdness
    
- 3 environmental threats
    
- 2 discoveries
    
- 1 social
