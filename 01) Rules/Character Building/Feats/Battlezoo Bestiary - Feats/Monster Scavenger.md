# Monster Scavenger
## Feat 1
### [[general]][[skill]]

**Category** skill; 



**Prerequisites** trained in Survival
* * *
You are particularly adept at using every part of the creatures you defeat. You can use Survival to Earn Income scavenging monster parts. If you choose to use an appropriate Lore skill instead, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to the check. The bonus increase to a +2 circumstance bonus if you're a master in Survival.
