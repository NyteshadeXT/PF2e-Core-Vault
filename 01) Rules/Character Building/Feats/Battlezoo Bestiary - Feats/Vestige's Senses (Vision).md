# Vestige's Senses (Vision)
## Feat 4
### [[archetype]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** you must defeat a monster with low-light vision or darkvision; Vestige Hunter Dedication
* * *
You incorporated a vestige from a monster with additional senses, granting you those you had not yet gained from your first vestiges. This often takes the form of the monster's eyes placed somewhere in your equipment. You gain darkvision and low-light vision.
