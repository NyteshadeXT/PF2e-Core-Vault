# Basic Oracle Spellcasting
## Feat 4
### [[archetype]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** Oracle Dedication
* * *
You gain the [[Compendium.pf2e.journals.JournalEntry.vx5FGEG34AxI2dow.JournalEntryPage.DOc3Pf8wmVxanTIv|Basic Spellcasting Feat]] benefits. When you gain a spell slot of a new rank from the oracle archetype, add a common divine spell or another divine spell you have learned or discovered to your repertoire, of that spell rank.
