# Shielding Strike  `pf2:1`  
## Focus 1
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[concentrate]][[focus]][[magus]]

* * * 
As you attack a foe, warding magic transforms your momentum into action and lifts your shield. Make a melee Strike. You can then either [[Actions/Raise a Shield|Raise a Shield]] if you're wielding one or cast [[Spells/Shield|Shield]] if you have the spell.

_Note: Conflux spells refresh Spellstrike._
