# Death Ward  `pf2:2`  
## Spell 5
### [[concentrate]][[manipulate]]
**Traditions** [[divine]], [[occult]], [[primal]]
**Range** touch
**Targets** 1 living creature touched
**Duration** 10 minutes
* * * 
You shield a creature from the ravages of void energy.

It receives a +4 status bonus to saves against death and void effects, gains void resistance 10, and suppresses the effects of the [[Conditions/Doomed 1|Doomed]] condition.
