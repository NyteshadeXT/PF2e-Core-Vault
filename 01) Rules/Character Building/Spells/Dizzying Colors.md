# Dizzying Colors  `pf2:2`  
## Spell 1
### [[concentrate]][[illusion]][[incapacitation]][[manipulate]][[visual]]
**Traditions** [[arcane]], [[occult]]
**Area** 15-foot cone
**Saving Throw**  will
**Duration** 1 or more rounds (see below)
* * * 
You unleash a swirling multitude of colors that overwhelms creatures based on their Will saves.

* * *

**Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.

**Success** The creature is [[Conditions/Dazzled|Dazzled]] for 1 round.

**Failure** The creature is [[Conditions/Stunned 1|Stunned 1]], [[Conditions/Blinded|Blinded]] for 1 round, and dazzled for 1 minute.

**Critical Failure** The creature is stunned for 1 round and blinded for 1 minute.
