# Cloak of Colors  `pf2:2`  
## Spell 5
### [[concentrate]][[illusion]][[manipulate]][[visual]]
**Traditions** [[arcane]], [[occult]]
**Range** 30 feet
**Targets** 1 creature
**Duration** 1 minute
* * * 
A cloak of swirling colors shrouds the target. Creatures are [[Conditions/Dazzled|Dazzled]] while adjacent to it, and attacking the target causes a brilliant flash of light. A creature that hits the target with a melee attack must attempt a Will save.The creature is temporarily immune until the end of its turn; this effect has the incapacitation trait.

* * *

**Success** The attacker is unaffected.

**Failure** The attacker is [[Conditions/Blinded|Blinded]] for 1 round.

**Critical Failure** The attacker is [[Conditions/Stunned 1|Stunned]] for 1 round.

*Source: Pathfinder Core Rulebook*
