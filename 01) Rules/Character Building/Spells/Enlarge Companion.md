# Enlarge Companion  `pf2:2`  
## Focus 2
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[concentrate]][[focus]][[manipulate]][[polymorph]][[ranger]]

**Range** 30 feet
**Targets** your animal companion
**Duration** 1 minute
* * * 
Your animal companion grows much larger, towering over its foes in battle. Your animal companion becomes Large, gaining the effects of a 2nd-rank [[Spells/Enlarge|Enlarge]] spell.

* * *

**Heightened (4th)** Your animal companion instead becomes Huge, gaining the benefits of a 4th-rank _enlarge_ spell
