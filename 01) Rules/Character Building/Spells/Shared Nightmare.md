# Shared Nightmare  `pf2:2`  
## Focus 4
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[cleric]][[concentrate]][[emotion]][[focus]][[incapacitation]][[manipulate]][[Mental]]

**Range** 30 feet
**Targets** 1 creature
**Saving Throw**  will
**Duration** varies
* * * 
Merging minds with the target, you swap distressing visions. One of you will become [[Conditions/Confused|Confused]], based on the target's Will save.

* * *

**Critical Success** You are Confused for 1 round.

**Success** At the start of your next turn, you spend your first action with the Confused condition, then act normally.

**Failure** The target spends the first action of each of its turns Confused. The duration is 1 minute.

**Critical Failure** The target is Confused. The duration is 1 minute.

*Source: Pathfinder Player Core*
