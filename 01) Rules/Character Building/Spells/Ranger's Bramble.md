# Ranger's Bramble  `pf2:2`  
## Focus 3
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[concentrate]][[focus]][[manipulate]][[plant]][[ranger]]

**Range** 100 feet
**Area** 5-foot burst
**Saving Throw**  reflex
**Duration** 1 minute
* * * 
You cause plants in the area to entangle your foes, with the effects of [[Spells/Entangling Flora|Entangling Flora]]. A creature that critically fails the save takes 2d4 bleed damage in addition to being [[Conditions/Immobilized|Immobilized]]. [[Actions/Escape|Escaping]] your bramble doesn't end the bleed damage.

* * *

**Heightened (+1)** The bleed damage on a critical failure increases by 1d4.
