# Intelligence
Divination

Magical

**Type** Skill Item (Intelligence-based skill)

**Parts** The creature must have Intelligence as its highest or second- highest ability modifier

* * *

**Effect** You imbue the item with brilliant intelligence.

*   **8th** The item can cast _Hypercognition_ once per day as an occult spell, as your inner Charisma inspires you or an ally to greatness
*   **14th** The item can cast _hypercognition_ once per hour instead of once per day
*   **17th** When you invest the item, you either increase your Intelligence score by 2 or increase it to 18, whichever would give you a higher score. The item gains the apex trait
*   **20th** The item can cast _hypercognition_ once per minute instead of once per hour.
