# Harvest Creature

### [[exploration]][[manipulate]]

**Category** interaction; 




**Requirements** You are holding or wearing a carver's kit.

* * *

You spend some time harvesting the hide, guts, teeth, claws, and other useful parts from a dead creature. The amount of time spent depends on the creature’s size, and you must use the carver’s kit with both hands. You roll a `Crafting check` check. The GM sets the DC, which is usually a [[PF2e Journals/GM Screen/standard DC for the creature’s level.|standard DC for the creature’s level.]]

To harvest a creature, you must meet the following requirements:

*   The level of creature you can harvest equals 0 + your proficiency bonus with the Crafting skill (your level + proficiency rank bonus). Any creatures of higher level are too complex for your skillset, and you cannot harvest them without botching the corpse in the process.
*   You must know what the creature is. If you do not know the creature, you don’t know its anatomy well enough to harvest it.
    

* * *

**Critical Success** You harvest the maximum possible amount of loot.

**Success** The GM rolls for loot in the creature entry. You harvest the amount of loot rolled. If a category of item only has one item, the GM doesn't roll and you are guaranteed that one item.

**Failure** The GM rolls for loot in the creature entry. You harvest half the amount of loot rolled, rounded down. If you would halve a single item, such as one dragon fang, the item is not harvested.

**Critical Failure** You botch the harvest. No loot is gained.
