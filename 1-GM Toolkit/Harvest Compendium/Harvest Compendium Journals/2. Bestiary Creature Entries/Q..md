# Q.
## [[Bestiary 1/Quelaunt|Quelaunt]]

Dread awaits those who harvest a quelaunt. A creature that attempts to harvest a quelaunt is targeted by the quelaunt’s curse. If the creature is successful in their check to harvest the creature and in their save against the curse, they may harvest the listed items in this entry as normal.

### Quelaunt’s Curse (Curse 15)

Curse

magical

mental

* * *

You are plagued by terrible visions and feel your life begin to lose all meaning. The written word loses its purpose and paranoia binds you like cold chains.

* * *

**Saving Throw** `DC 32 Will check`

**Effect** Reality turns into a living nightmare. You cannot succeed or critically succeed on saving throws against mental effects or on Charisma-based skill checks. Any time you attempt to write something, it comes out as illegible scribbles to others, though it appears legible to you. You are also drawn towards death. In combat, you must use your first action on your turn to Stride as close to a hostile creature as you can get. If you are already within 5 feet of a hostile creature, you instead waste the action, and you suffer a −5 circumstance penalty to AC until the beginning of your next turn. Every 7 days, you receive a new saving throw against the curse. The curse does not affect this saving throw.
