# Harvest Compendium
Adventurers brave the dangers of the world, and many of those dangers have teeth. Surviving a fire-breathing dragon or the piercing wail of a banshee leaves you with a great story to tell—but what if you want more than that? This compendium supplies a list of loot that a band of brave adventurers can harvest from the corpses of the monsters they slay.

Though this compendium has plenty of loot to offer, the listed items don’t represent every instance of a creature. A dragon might have lost an eye battling a previous adventure group and therefore only one eye can be harvested, or the creature may be wielding items in addition to those listed in its stat block. The GM can always change the available loot found on a creature, either by removing or adding items, to better fit their campaign.
