# Sea Serpent Blood
## Item 1
### [[ingredient]]


**Price** 1 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** other

This blood appears blue in its vial and turns red when exposed to air for longer than a minute. The blood can be used to craft a [[Equipment/Sea Touch Elixir (Lesser)|Sea Touch Elixir]] or a [[Equipment/Potion of Swimming|Potion of Swimming]].
