# Shadow Shift Cloak
## Item 5
### [[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 150 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** worncloak

This cloak is so dark that it blends in with the shadows. While wearing the cloak, you are [[Conditions/Concealed|Concealed]] when you stand in dim light, even to creatures with low-light vision or darkvision.

* * *

**Craft Requirements** The initial raw materials must include one cloaker hide.
