# Shuln Claws
## Item 9
### [[creature-component]]


**Price** 250 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** other

This claw is made of adamantine. The claw can be used to craft a weapon from the knife weapon group. The weapon has a [[Equipment/Striking (Greater)|Striking (Greater)]] rune etched into it. The weapon also has the following feature.

**Armor-Rending** When you score a critical hit with a melee Strike using this weapon, you deal the same amount of damage to the target's armor, bypassing any Hardness lower than 10.
