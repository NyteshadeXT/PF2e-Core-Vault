# Shifter Solution
## Item 1
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[consumable]][[magical]][[potion]]

**Category** potion; 
**Price** 2 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:1` Interact

* * *

This liquid seems unable to settle in its vial, constantly twisting in shape. Drinking it allows you to subtly change your features, such as eye color, hair color, and facial features. You gain a +1 item bonus to Deception checks, and a +2 item bonus to checks made to Impersonate. While under the effects of the solution, you don’t need a disguise kit to Impersonate.

The solution lasts for 10 minutes, after which it leaves your system. If you drink another dose of the solution within 1 minute of drinking the first, the duration is extended to 1 hour.
