# Shield Archon Eyes
## Item 8
### [[consumable]][[creature-component]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 30 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:2` Interact

* * *

This eye is white, as if blind to ther world. When you activate the eye, you crush it and cast [[Spells/Translocate|Dimension Door]]. When you cast the spell this way, you can choose to move into the space of a willing ally you can see within range of the spell. If you do so, your ally switches places with you, appearing in the space you just vacated as if the ally had also cast _Translocate._
