# Sylvan Wineskin
## Item 4
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[consumable]][[magical]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 20 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

This wineskin is filled with enchanted alcohol a satyr left inside. When you drink from the wineskin, you gain a +1 item bonus to Will saves for 1 hour. This bonus is increased to +2 against fear effects. The wineskin holds 2d4 drafts of wine when harvested. When the last draft is consumed, the wineskin loses its magic.
