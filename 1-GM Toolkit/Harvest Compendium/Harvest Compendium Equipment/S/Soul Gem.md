# Soul Gem
## Item 1
### [[creature-component]][[trophy]]


**Price** 10 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** other

This soul gem is filled with a creature's soul, the type of which determines its value. The information listed provides the price for a soul gem and the level to guide when an adventuring group may find a soul gem of that quality.

*   Mindless Spirit (ooze, zombie); **Level** 1, **Price** 10 gp,
*   Animal Spirit (horse, wolf); **Level** 2-3, **Price** 25 gp,
*   Basic Soul (humanoid commoner); **Level** 4-7, **Price** 100 gp,
*   Noteworthy Soul (adventurer, drake); **Level** 8-14, **Price** 500 gp,
*   Grand Soul (dragon, powerful hero); **Level** 15+, **Price** 1000-5000 gp
