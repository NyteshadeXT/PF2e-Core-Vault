# Stuffed Spider Doll
## Item 8
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[consumable]][[magical]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 75 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-two-hands

Activate `pf2:2` Interact

* * *

This small stuffed spider is surprisingly detailed. You activate the spider by tearing it in two and throwing the pieces at a clear space within 10 feet of you. A [[Monster Core/Giant Tarantula|Giant Tarantula]] is summoned in the space. The spider has the summoned trait and remains for 10 minutes or until reduced to 0 Hit Points, whichever comes first. You do not need to Sustain a Spell to direct these summoned creatures.
