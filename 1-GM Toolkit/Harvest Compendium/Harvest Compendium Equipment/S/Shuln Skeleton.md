# Shuln Skeleton
## Item 10
### [[trophy]]


**Price** 2000 gp; 
**Bulk** 10; **Size** med
**Usage** other

The skeleton of a shuln is made of adamantine and is incredibly rare. It can be sold to collectors, or to artisans working with adamantine. If the skeleton is harvested, you cannot harvest any fangs or claws from the creature.
