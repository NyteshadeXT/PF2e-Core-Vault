# Skeletal Lore Necklace
## Item 11
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 1400 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** wornnecklace

While worn, you can tap into the memories of the creatures whose bones make up this necklace. This grants you trained proficiency in the Skeletal Lore skill. In addition, you can speak and understand all the languages known by the creatures whose bones make up the necklace. The GM decides the languages granted by the bones.
