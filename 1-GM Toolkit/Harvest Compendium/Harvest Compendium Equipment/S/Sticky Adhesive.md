# Sticky Adhesive
## Item 3
### [[consumable]][[magical]][[oil]]

**Category** oil; 
**Price** 10 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-two-hands

Activate `pf2:1` Interact

* * *

This adhesive is sticky and stringy. The adhesive can be applied to armor. Any creature that touches you or hits you with a melee Strike must succeed at a `DC 18 Reflex check` save or become [[Conditions/Grabbed|Grabbed]] by the adhesive until it [[Actions/escape dc=18|escape dc=18]]{Escapes} (DC 18). A weapon that hits you is stuck to you and can be removed with a successful `DC 18 Athletics check` check as a single action with the manipulate trait. Items adhered to you count towards your Bulk limits. The adhesive dissolves 1 minute after application, releasing all stuck objects and creatures.
