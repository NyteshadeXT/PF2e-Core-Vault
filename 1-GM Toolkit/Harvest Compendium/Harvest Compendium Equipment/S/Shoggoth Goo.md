# Shoggoth Goo
## Item 15
### [[consumable]][[creature-component]][[edible]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 1200 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:1` Interact

* * *

This goo is blackish green, with small specks of glowing green floating within. When you consume this goo, you must succeed at a `DC 34 Will check` save or become [[Conditions/Confused|Confused]] for 1 round (or 2d4 rounds on a critical failure). If you successfully save, you gain resistance 15 to acid and cold damage for 1 minute.
