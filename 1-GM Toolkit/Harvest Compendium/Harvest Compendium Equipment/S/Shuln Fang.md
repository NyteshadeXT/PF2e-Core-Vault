# Shuln Fang
## Item 9
### 


**Price** 275 gp; 
**Bulk** 1; **Size** med
**Usage** other

This fang is made of adamantine. The fang can be used to craft a weapon from the hammer or sword weapon groups. The weapon has a [[Equipment/Striking (Greater)|Striking (Greater)]] rune etched into it. The weapon also has the following feature.

**Armor-Rending** When you score a critical hit with a melee Strike using this weapon, you deal the same amount of damage to the target's armor, bypassing any Hardness lower than 10.
