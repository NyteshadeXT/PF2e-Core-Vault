# Signet Ring

### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")


**Price** 3 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** wornring

This signet ring has the crest of a noble family on it. The signet is designed to be pushed into wax to make wax seals on letters. While wearing this ring, you gain a +2 item bonus to Deception checks made to pass yourself off as a member of the relevant noble family.
