# Smilodon Fangs
## Item 2
### [[creature-component]]


**Price** 15 gp; 
**Bulk** 1; **Size** med
**Usage** other

This smilodon fang is large and sharp. A fang can be used to craft a weapon from the knife group. This weapon has an increased damage dice, dealing 1d6 damage instead of 1d4. 1d8 damage instead of 1d6, and so on (to a maximum of 1d12). It also has the backstabber trait.
