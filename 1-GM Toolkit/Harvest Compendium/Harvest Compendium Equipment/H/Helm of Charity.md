# Helm of Charity
## Item 4
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 75 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** wornheadwear

**Activate** `pf2:1` manipulate

* * *

When you activate this helm, you touch another creature and take on that creature’s wounds, transferring up to 25 of your Hit Points to the touched creature. You cannot transfer more Hit Points than you currently have.

* * *

**Craft Requirements** The initial raw materials must include two horned archon antlers.
