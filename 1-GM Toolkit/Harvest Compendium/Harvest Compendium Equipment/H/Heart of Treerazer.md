# Heart of Treerazer
## Item 9
### [[consumable]][[creature-component]][[edible]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 150 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:2` Interact

* * *

This heart has a foul stench and oozes brackish blood. It glows with a faint greenish light. After consuming the heart, you are filled with fiendish energy. Once within the next 7 days, you can cast the [[Spells/Demonic Pact|Abyssal Pact]] ritual even if you aren't a demon. Eating the heart does not give you knowledge of the ritual. You must still know the ritual in order to cast it, and all other requirements of the ritual must still apply.
