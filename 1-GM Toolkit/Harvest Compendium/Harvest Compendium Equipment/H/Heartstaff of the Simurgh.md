This staff has the petrified heart of a simurgh as its crown. When used as a weapon, a heartstaff of the simurgh is a +2 greater striking staff. While wielding it, you gain +1 item bonus to all saves against magic.

Voluntarily destroying a heartstaff calls a torrent of flame into existence. While wielding the staff, you can use an Interact action to break it. This releases a 30-foot burst magical explosion centered on the staff. The explosion deals 2d8 fire damage per charge remaining in the staff (`DC 40 Reflex check` save). You automatically critically fail your save. Creatures that are immune to fire, instead of gaining the usual benefit of immunity, treat the results of their saving throws as one degree of success better. A creature reduced to 0 Hit Points by this damage dies instantly; this is a death effect.
