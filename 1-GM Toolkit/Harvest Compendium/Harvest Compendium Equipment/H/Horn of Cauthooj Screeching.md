# Horn of Cauthooj Screeching
## Item 8
### [[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 450 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

This long horn is decorated with cauthooj feathers. It can be played as an instrument, granting a +2 item bonus to Performance checks.

**Activate** `pf2:2` manipulate (auditory, incapacitation, mental, primal)

**Frequency** once per hour

**Effect** You blow in the horn and create an ululating cry, turning nearby creatures wild and violent. Each creature within a 60-foot emanation that can hear the horn other than you must attempt a `DC 24 Will check` save to resist the effect.

* * *

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected and is temporarily immune for 24 hours.

**Success** The target is unaffected.

**Failure** The target is [[Conditions/Confused|Confused]] for 1 round.

**Critical Failure** The target is confused for 1 round and immediately attacks itself (in the normal fashion for attacking oneself while confused). This Strike doesn’t give the creature a flat check to recover from being confused.

* * *

**Craft Requirements** The initial raw materials must include one set of cauthooj vocal cords
