# Hourglass of Lesser Death
## Item 15
### [unique](unique "Unique Rarity Trait")[[consumable]][[magical]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 1200 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:1` Interact

* * *

When you activate this hourglass, you turn it and let the sands of mortal time fall. For 1 minute, you automatically know the Hit Points, conditions, afflictions, and emotions of all creatures you can see. When the effect ends, the sand runs out and the hourglass cracks into pieces. You lose all of your Hit Points and fall unconscious and are dying 2. You add any wounded value you may have to this dying condition as normal.
