# Hag Coven Amulet

### [[trophy]]


**Price** 10 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** other

Coven amulets don't necessarily mean the hag is a member of a coven, but they serve as a symbol of the destruction a gathering of hags could cause. Hags without covens usually have a blank pendant on their amulet, while coven-bound hags have a symbol representing their union engraved on the pendant.
