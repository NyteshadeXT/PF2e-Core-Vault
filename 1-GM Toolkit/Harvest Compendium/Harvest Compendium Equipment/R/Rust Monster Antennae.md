# Rust Monster Antennae
## Item 3
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[consumable]][[magical]][[talisman]]

**Category** talisman; 
**Price** 10 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** affixed-to-weapon

**Activate** `pf2:0` envision

**Trigger** You successfully Strike a creature wearing or holding an item made of metal.

* * *

This twitching antenna is covered in a layer of red rust. When you activate the antenna, you deal 2d6 damage (doubled on a critical hit) to a metal item the target is wearing or carrying, ignoring its Hardness. If you hit an unattended metal item, the item takes this damage automatically. If the targeted creature uses the [[Bestiary Ability Glossary/Shield Block|Shield Block]] reaction with a metal shield against the attack, the shield is automatically [[Conditions/Broken|Broken]], but no other item is rusted
