# Roper Strands
## Item 2
### [[creature-component]]


**Price** 15 gp; 
**Bulk** 1; **Size** med
**Usage** other

These strands occasionally constrict on their own. A strand can be used to craft a weapon from the flail weapon group. The weapon has a [[Equipment/Weapon Potency (+1)|Weapon Potency (+1)]] rune etched into it, and it has the grapple weapon trait if it doesn't already.
