# Guthallath Eyes
## Item 16
### [rare](rare "Rare Rarity Trait")[[consumable]][[creature-component]][[magical]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 1800 gp; 
**Bulk** 2; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-two-hands

**Activate** `pf2:2` Interact

* * *

You can hold up this eye and point it at your enemies to activate the destructive magic within. When activated, a 120-foot line erupts from the eye. Everything in this line is automatically hit by an 8th-rank [[Spells/Disintegrate|Disintegrate]] spell (spell DC 35).

This cumbersome eye was never meant to be wielded in this way. When you activate the eye, you must make a `DC 35 Fortitude check`.

* * *

**Success** You keep the line steady.

**Failure** You lose your grip and the eye jitters, disrupting the line. Creatures that would be affected by the line gain a +5 circumstance bonus to their Fortitude save against the line's effects.

**Critical Failure** The eye slips from your hands and fails, pointing directly at you. The line hits you instead of your intended targets.
