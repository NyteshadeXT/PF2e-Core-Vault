# Ghost Bane Oil
## Item 4
### [[consumable]][[magical]][[oil]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 15 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-two-hands

**Activate** `pf2:1` Interact

* * *

This oil is thick and iridescent. The oil can be applied to a melee weapon or to five pieces of ammunition. For 1 minute, the weapon or ammunition can harm incorporeal creatures, as though it had ghost touch rune etched into it.

[[Harvest Compendium Effects/Effect_ Ghost Bane Oil|Effect: Ghost Bane Oil]]

* * *

**Craft Requirements** The initial crafting materials must include one vial of ectoplasm.
