# Ghaele Eye
## Item 13
### [[consumable]][[creature-component]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 600 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:2` Interact

* * *

This eye glows a bright green. When you activate the eye, you crush it and cast [[Spells/Divine Decree|Divine Decree]] using your spellcasting ability and without expending a spell slot. You must be a divine spellcaster to use the component in this way and have a spell slot of the appropriate level, but you do not need to know the spell or have it prepared.
