# Cinder Smog Cannister
## Item 3
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[consumable]][[magical]][[talisman]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 10 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** affixed-to-armor

**Activate** `pf2:r` Interact

**Trigger** You take damage from a hostile source.

* * *

The smog in this vial is gray and thick with small flecks of embers within. When you take damage, you can break the vial and unleash the smog. The smog fills a 15-foot radius burst centered on one corner of your space. All creatures within that area are concealed, and all other creatures are [[Conditions/Concealed|Concealed]] to them. Any creature that starts its turn within the smoke takes 2d6 fire damage. The smoke lasts for 1 minute or until dispersed by a strong wind.
