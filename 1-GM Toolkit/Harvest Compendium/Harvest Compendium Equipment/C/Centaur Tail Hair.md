# Centaur Tail Hair
## Item 1
### [[ingredient]]


**Price** 1 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** other

These tufts of hair can be used to craft a [[Equipment/Climbing Bolt|Climbing Bolt]] or a [[Equipment/Rope of Climbing (Lesser)|Rope of Climbing]]. If used as an additional component when crafting a snare, the cost of the total required raw materials is lowered by 10%. Only one tuft or tail hair can be used in this manner per snare.
