# Cloak of Flying
## Item 8
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 500 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** worncloak

This cloak has the shape of large wings. It flutters gently, as if a wind is blowing against it.

* * *

**Activate** `pf2:1` concentrate

**Frequency** once per day

**Effect** You gain a fly Speed of 30 feet for 10 minutes.
