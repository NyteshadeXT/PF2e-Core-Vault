# Curse-Breaker Claws
## Item 5
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[consumable]][[magical]][[talisman]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 25 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** affixed-to-armor

**Activate** `pf2:r` envision

**Trigger** You are targeted by a curse and must make a saving throw

**Requirements** You are an expert in Occultism.

* * *

Touching this claw for more than a few minutes causes immense discomfort. You gain a +5 item bonus against the effects of the curse. If using the claw causes you to succeed on the save against the curse, you suffer some penalties (see table below). If you critically succeed, you suffer no penalties.

[[Harvest Compendium Effects/Effect_ Curse-Breaker Claws|Effect: Curse-Breaker Claws]]

  

| Curse Level | Effects |
| --- | --- |
| 1-4 | You take 1d6 mental damage |
| 5-9 | You take 2d6 mental damage. You are [[Conditions/Clumsy\|Clumsy 1]] for 1 hour. |
| 10-14 | You take 4d6 mental damage. You are [[Conditions/Clumsy\|Clumsy 2]] for 1 hour, and you are [[Conditions/Confused\|Confused]] |
| 15+ | You take 6d6 mental damage. You are [[Conditions/Clumsy\|Clumsy 3]] for 1 hour, and you are Confused. |
