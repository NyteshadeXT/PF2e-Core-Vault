# Cursed Sand
## Item 5
### [[consumable]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 0 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

This sand looks like regular sand, but when it settles, eerie images form on its surface. You can activate the sand by blowing it into the face of a living creature within 5 feet of you. The creature must make a `DC 20 Fortitude check` save against a curse.

* * *

**Critical Success** The creature is unaffected and becomes immune to cursed sand for 24 hours.

**Success** The creature is unaffected.

**Failure** The creature takes 2d6 void damage and can't regain hit points for the next hour.

**Critical Failure** The creature takes 4d6 void damage and can't regain hit points for the next hour.
