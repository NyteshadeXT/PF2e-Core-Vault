# Cloak of Flexibility
## Item 6
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 250 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** worncloak

This stretchy cloak can be folded very thin.

* * *

**Activate** `pf2:1` Interact

**Frequency** once per day

**Effect** For the next 10 minutes, you can fit through tight spaces as if you were a Small creature and can [[Actions/Squeeze|Squeeze]] at your full Speed.

* * *

**Craft Requirements** The initial raw materials must include two sections of gug rawhide.
