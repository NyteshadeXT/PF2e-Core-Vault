# Ceustodaemon Blood
## Item 4
### [[consumable]][[creature-component]][[edible]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 14 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:1` Interact

* * *

The blood is so dark it's almost black. After consuming the blood, you are filled with vicious rage that shows in your attacks. For 1 minute, when you hit a creature with a successful Strike, the target takes an additional 1d6 precision damage. You also take this additional damage. If this extra damage is doubled due to a critical hit, the damage you take is doubled as well.
