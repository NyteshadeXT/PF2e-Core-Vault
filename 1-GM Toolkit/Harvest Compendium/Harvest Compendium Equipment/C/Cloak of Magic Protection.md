# Cloak of Magic Protection
## Item 6
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 260 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** worncloak

This cloak looks like a tiger hide. While wearing the cloak, you gain a +2 item bonus to all saves against magic, and a +3 item bonus to all saves against divine magic.

* * *

**Craft Requirements** The initial raw materials must include two sections of Rakshasa rawhide.
