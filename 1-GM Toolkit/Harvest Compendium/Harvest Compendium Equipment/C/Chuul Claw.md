# Chuul Claw
## Item 6
### [[creature-component]]


**Price** 30 gp; 
**Bulk** 1; **Size** med
**Usage** other

This claw is dense and hard. A claw can be used to craft a weapon from the club or hammer weapon groups. The weapon has a [[Equipment/Striking|Striking]] rune etched into it. This weapon also has an increased damage dice, dealing 1d6 damage instead of 1d4, 1d8 damage instead of 1d6, and so on (to a maximum of 1d12). It also has the grapple weapon trait if it doesn't already.
