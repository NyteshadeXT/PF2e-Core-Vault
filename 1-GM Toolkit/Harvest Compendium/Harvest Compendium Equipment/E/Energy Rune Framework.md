# Energy Rune Framework
## Item 8
### [[consumable]][[creature-component]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 50 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** other

This framework outlines the steps to install a [[Equipment/Corrosive|Corrosive]], [[Equipment/Flaming|Flaming]], [[Equipment/Frost|Frost]] or [[Equipment/Shock|Shock]] rune. The type of rune is determined by the graveknight's life or death. When you etch the striking rune into a weapon, its price is lowered by 25%.
