# Empty Scroll Case

### 


**Price** 1 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-two-hands

This scroll case has a leather strap and hangs from your shoulder or over your chest. It can hold four scrolls or other pieces of parchment inside it. You can [[Actions/Interact|Interact]] with the case to stow items in it or remove them.
