# Terotricus Infestation Spores
## Item 6
### [[consumable]][[creature-component]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 200 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:1` Interact

* * *

You sprinkle the infestation spores on the ground at your feet, causing the spores to infest the nearby landscape. The spores drain nutrients from nearby trees and undergrowth while simultaneously infesting them with fungal growth. All non-magical plant life (though not plant creatures) within a 30-foot emanation withers and sprouts foul mold and slimy mushrooms, removing any cover and concealment provided by trees and undergrowth. The mold and mushrooms cannot survive without a terotricus near, withering into rot over the course of 10 minutes.
