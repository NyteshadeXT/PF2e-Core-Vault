# Textbook

### 


**Price** 5 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

If you spend 1 hour studying this book, you gain a further understanding of whatever topic is covered in the textbook (as detrmined by the GM). You gain a +2 item bonus to [[Actions/Recall Knowledge|Recall Knowledge]] checks about that specific topic. This bonus lasts for a number of hours equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of 1).
