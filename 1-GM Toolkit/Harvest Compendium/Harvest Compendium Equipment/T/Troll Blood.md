# Troll Blood
## Item 1
### [[ingredient]]


**Price** 2 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** other

This blood coagulates rapidly if taken out of its vial. The blood can be used to craft an [[Equipment/Elixir of Life (Minor)|Elixir of Life]], a [[Equipment/Healing Potion (Minor)|Healing Potion]], or a [[Feats/Smoke Bomb|Smoke Bomb]] (as in the alchemist feat). Smoke bombs created with troll blood are more potent than regular smoke bombs, as the affected creature suggers the [[Conditions/Confused|Confused]] condition in addition to any other effects.
