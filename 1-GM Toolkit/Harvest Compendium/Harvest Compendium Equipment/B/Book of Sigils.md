# Book of Sigils
## Item 1
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")


**Price** 10 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

The book is filled with indecipherable sigils and symbols. Once per day, you can spend 1 minute consulting the book when trying to make a [[Actions/Recall Knowledge|Recall Knowledge]] check using Occultism to gain a +2 item bonus to the check.
