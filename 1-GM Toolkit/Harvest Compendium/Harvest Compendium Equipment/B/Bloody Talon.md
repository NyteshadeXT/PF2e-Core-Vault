# Bloody Talon
## Item 1
### [[consumable]][[magical]][[talisman]]

**Category** talisman; 
**Price** 5 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** affixed-to-weapon

**Activate** `pf2:0` envision

**Trigger** You successfully Strike a creature with the affixed weapon that can bleed and hasn’t taken damage.

This talon is decorated with red symbols. Sometimes, the talon is exchanged for a suitable tooth or claw. When you activate the talon, you deal an additional 1d4 persistent bleed damage to the creature.
