# Oil of Fire Coating
## Item 3
### [[consumable]]

**Category** oil; 
**Price** 10 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-two-hands

Activate `pf2:1` Interact

* * *

This oil glows with the heat and light of flame. The oil can be applied to a melee weapon made of metal or to five pieces of ammunition. For 1 minute, the weapon or ammunition deals an additional 1d6 fire damage on a hit.
