# Oil of the Bastion
## Item 4
### [[consumable]][[earth]][[magical]][[oil]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 15 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-two-hands

Activate `pf2:2` Interact

* * *

This oil has little bits of stone in it. When you apply the oil to armor, the creature wearing the armor gains a +2 circumstance bonus to Reflex saves for 1 hour. The creature can only use this bonus if it hasn’t taken a move action since the beginning of its last turn.
