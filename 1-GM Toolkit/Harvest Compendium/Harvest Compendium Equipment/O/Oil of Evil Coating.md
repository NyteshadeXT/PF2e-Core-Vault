# Oil of Evil Coating
## Item 3
### [[consumable]][[magical]][[oil]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 10 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-two-hands

**Activate** `pf2:1` Interact

* * *

This oil is black and thick like tar. The oil can be applied to a melee weapon or to five pieces of ammunition. For 1 minute, the weapon or ammunition deals an additional 1d6 unholy spirit damage on a hit.
