# Oblivion Extract
## Item 5
### [[consumable]][[magical]][[oil]]

**Category** oil; 
**Price** 25 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-two-hands

Activate `pf2:1` Interact

* * *

This extract is a deep black and has a putrid smell. You can spread this liquid over a non-magical item of 1 Bulk or less. After 1 minute, the item is destroyed, leaving no trace of its existence.
