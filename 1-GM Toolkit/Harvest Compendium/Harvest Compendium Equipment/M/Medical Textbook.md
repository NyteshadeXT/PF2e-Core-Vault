# Medical Textbook

### 


**Price** 0 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

If you spend 1 hour studying this book, you gain a further understanding of basic field medicine and humanoid physiology. You gain a +2 item bonus to Medicine checks. This bonus lasts for a number of hours equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of 1).
