# Minotaur Nose Ring
## Item 3
### [[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 45 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** wornring

This ring carries some of the power of the mighty minotaur. While wearing this ring, you can choose which aspect of the minotaur to embody. During your daily preparations, you choose whether you gain a +2 item bonus to avoid becoming lost and to find your way, or a +2 item bonus to Intimidation checks made to Demoralize. The chosen benefit lasts until your next daily preparations.
