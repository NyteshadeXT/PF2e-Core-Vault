# Pleroma Energy Cloak
## Item 12
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 1800 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** worncloak

This energy cloak shimmers with the power of the cosmos, the sight of which is hidden within its folds. While wearing the cloak, you ignore effects that would give you a circumstance penalty to your Speeds.

* * *

**Activate** `pf2:1` envision

**Frequency** once per day

**Effect** You gain a fly Speed of 40 feet for 10 minutes.

[[Harvest Compendium Effects/Effect_ Pleroma Energy Cloak|Effect: Pleroma Energy Cloak]]

* * *

**Activate** `pf2:r` command

**Frequency** once per hour

**Trigger** You critically fail a saving throw

**Effect** The critical failure becomes a failure instead.

* * *

**Craft Requirements** The initial raw materials must include one section of pleroma energy fabric.
