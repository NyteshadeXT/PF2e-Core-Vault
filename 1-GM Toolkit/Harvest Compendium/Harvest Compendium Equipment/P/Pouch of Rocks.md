# Pouch of Rocks

### 


**Price** 5 cp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** other

These rocks are practically worthless, but they are prettily decorated with symbols and carvings, making them nice to look at. You gain a +1 item bonus to checks that use the rocks as part of a performance or disguise, such as pretending the rocks have magical properties.
