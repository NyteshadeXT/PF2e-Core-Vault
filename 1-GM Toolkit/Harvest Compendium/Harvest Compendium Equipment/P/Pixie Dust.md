# Pixie Dust
## Item 3
### [[consumable]][[creature-component]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 10 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-two-hands

**Activate** `pf2:1` Interact

* * *

You can sprinkle a dose of pixie dust onto a piece of ammunition. If you hit a creature with that piece of ammunition before your next turn, the ammunition inflicts one of the following special effects instead of dealing damage. Each effects depends on the target's Will save. On a critical hit, treat the save result as one degree worse.

*   **Charm** (emotion, incapacitation, mental) The targets suffers the effects of a charm spell, except it doesn't gain a bonus to its save if the only hostile act was you hitting it with a piece of pixie dust ammunition, and you can choose to direct the target's adoration toward another creature rather than yourself. `DC 17 Will check`
*   **Memory Loss** (mental) The target loses the last 5 minutes of its memory. `DC 17 Will check`
*   **Sleep** (incapacitation, mental, sleep) The target suffers the effects of a 3rd-level [[Spells/Sleep|Sleep]] spell. `DC 17 Will check`
*   **Subdual** (mental, nonlethal) The target takes 4d6 mental damage, depending on its `DC 17 Will check`
