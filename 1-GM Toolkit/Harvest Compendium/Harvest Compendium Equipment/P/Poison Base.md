# Poison Base
## Item 3
### [[ingredient]]


**Price** 10 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** other

This poison only causes short-term nausea on its own but can be crafted into something more powerful. If used as an additional component when crafting a poison, the cost of total required raw materials is lowered by 10%. Up to three vials of poison base can be used when crafting a poison, for a maximum 30% discount on materials.
