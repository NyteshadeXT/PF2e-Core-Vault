# Poetry Book

### 


**Price** 5 sp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

If you spend 1 hour studying this book, you can practice the rhythms and cadence of the poetry within. You gain a +2 item bonus to Performance checks made to recite poetry. This bonus lasts for a number of hours equal to your Intelligence modifier.
