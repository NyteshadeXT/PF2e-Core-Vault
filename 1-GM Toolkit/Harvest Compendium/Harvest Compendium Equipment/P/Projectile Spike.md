# Projectile Spike
## Item 2
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[consumable]][[magical]][[talisman]]

**Category** talisman; 
**Price** 5 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** affixed-to-weapon

**Activate** `pf2:0` envision

**Trigger** You successfully Strike a creature with the affixed weapon, and the target is within 5 feet of at least one other creature hostile to you.

* * *

The spike is sharp and slightly curved. When you activate the spike, it shoots away from your weapon and hits up to two hostile creatures within 5 feet of the target of your Strike. These additional targets take 1d10 piercing damage.
