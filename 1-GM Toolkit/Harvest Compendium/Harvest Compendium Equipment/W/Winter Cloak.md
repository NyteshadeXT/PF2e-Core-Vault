# Winter Cloak
## Item 5
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 150 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** worncloak

This cloak thick and heavy. While wearing the cloak, you are protected from cold temperatures up to incredible cold.

* * *

**Activate** `pf2:r` envision

**Frequency** once per day

**Trigger** You take cold damage

**Effect** You gain resistance 15 to the triggering damage.
