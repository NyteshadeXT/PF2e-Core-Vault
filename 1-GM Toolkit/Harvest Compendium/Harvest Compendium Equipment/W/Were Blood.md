# Were Blood
## Item 3
### [[consumable]][[creature-component]][[edible]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 10 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:1` Interact

* * *

This blood shimmers when touched by moonlight. After consuming this blood, you can speak with animals, as with the [[Spells/Speak with Animals|Speak with Animals]] spell. The type of animal you can speak to is limited to animals of the same general kind as the werecreature the blood was harvested from.
