This ink is pitch black and has the consistency of gel. It can only be used underwater. On a hit, ink fills a 20-foot emanation centered on what it struck. Creatures inside the cloud are exposed to the kraken ink poison and are [[Conditions/Undetected|Undetected]] while inside the cloud.

* * *

**Kraken Ink** (poison); Krakens are immune to this poison.

**Saving Throw** `DC 39 Fortitude check`

**Maximum Duration** 10 rounds

**Stage 1** 3d6 poison damage and [[Conditions/Sickened|Sickened 1]] (1 round)

**Stage 2** 4d6 poison damage and [[Conditions/Sickened|Sickened 2]] (1 round)
