# Long-Range Ammunition
## Item 2
### [[consumable]][[magical]]


**Price** 5 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** carried

**Ammunition** arrow, bolt

* * *

This ammunition can fly further than regular ammunition. When fired, the attack’s range increments increase in length by 30 feet.

* * *

**Craft Requirements** The initial crafting materials must include two eagle-feathers or other suitable feathers (see [[Harvest Compendium Journals/2. Bestiary Creature Entries|Bestiary Creature Entries]])
