# Darkness Grenade (Moderate)
## Item 7
### [[alchemical]][[consumable]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 60 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

When you activate this grenade, you bounce it along the floor until it comes to rest in an unoccupied space within 20 feet of you. A thick 15-foot-radius shroud of darkness erupts from the shell, preventing light from penetrating or emanating within the area, like with the [[Spells/Darkness|Darkness]] spell. The darkness lasts for 1d4 + 1 rounds.

Even creatures with darkvision (but not greater darkvision) can barely see through the darkness. Creatures with darkvision treat targets seen through the darkness as concealed.
