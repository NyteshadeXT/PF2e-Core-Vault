# Dragon Fangs (Blue or Bronze)
## Item 9
### [[creature-component]]


**Price** 75 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** other

This fang occasionally produces an electrical charge. A fang can be used to craft a weapon that deals piercing damage. The weapon is a _+1_ [[Equipment/Shock|Shock]] weapon. This weapon also has an increased damage dice, dealing 1d6 damage instead of 1d4, 1d8 instead of 1d6, and so on (to a maximum of 1d12).
