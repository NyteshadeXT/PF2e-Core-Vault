# Draconic Ammunition (Silver or White)
## Item 2
### [[consumable]][[magical]]


**Price** 5 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** carried

**Ammunition** arrow, bolt

* * *

A creature hit with this ammunition takes 1d6 damage of a type matching the dragon's color (see the table below).

* * *

**Craft Requirements** The initial crafting materials must include one dragon tooth or other suitable tooth (see creature entries).

  

| Dragon Type | Damage Type |
| --- | --- |
| Black or Copper | Acid |
| Blue or Brass | Electricity |
| Brass, gold, or red | Fire |
| Green | Poison |
| Silver or white | Cold |
