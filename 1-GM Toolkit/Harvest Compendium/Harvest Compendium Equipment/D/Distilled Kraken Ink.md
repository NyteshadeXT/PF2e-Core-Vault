# Distilled Kraken Ink
## Item 16
### [[consumable]][[magical]][[talisman]]

**Category** talisman; 
**Price** 2000 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** affixed-to-armor

**Activate** `pf2:r` Interact

**Trigger** You take damage from a hostile source and you are underwater

**Requirements** You have a swim speed.

* * *

This ink is pitch black and has the consistency of gel. When you take damage, you can break the vial and unleash the ink. The ink fills a 20-foot emanation centered on you. Creatures inside the cloud are exposed to kraken ink poison and are [[Conditions/Undetected|Undetected]] while inside the cloud. The ink lasts for 1 minute or until dispersed by a strong current.

* * *

**Kraken Ink** (poison); Krakens are immune to this poison.

**Saving Throw** `DC 39 Fortitude check`

**Maximum Duration** 10 rounds

**Stage 1** 3d6 poison damage and [[Conditions/Sickened|Sickened 1]] (1 round)

**Stage 2** 4d6 poison damage and [[Conditions/Sickened|Sickened 2]] (1 round)
