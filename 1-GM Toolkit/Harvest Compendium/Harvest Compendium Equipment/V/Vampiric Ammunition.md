# Vampiric Ammunition
## Item 3
### [[consumable]][[magical]]


**Price** 15 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** carried

**Ammunition** arrow, bolt

* * *

A creature hit with this ammunition takes an additional 2d4 void damage. You gain temporary Hit Points equal to half the void damage the target takes (after applying resistances and the like). The temporary Hit Points last for 1 minute.

* * *

**Craft Requirements** The initial crafting materials must include one vampire fang.
