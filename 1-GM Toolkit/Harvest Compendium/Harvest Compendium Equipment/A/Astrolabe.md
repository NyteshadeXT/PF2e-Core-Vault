# Astrolabe
## Item 1
### 


**Price** 10 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-two-hands

A creature trained in Survival can use the astrolabe to assist with observing the night sky for information. While using the astrolabe, you gain a +1 item bonus to checks made to identify celestial bodies and to navigate using the stars.
