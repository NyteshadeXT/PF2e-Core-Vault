# Astradaemon Tail
## Item 12
### [[creature-component]]


**Price** 350 gp; 
**Bulk** 1; **Size** med
**Usage** other

This tail is long and slender. A tail can be used to craft a [[Equipment/Whip|Whip]]. The weapon has a [[Equipment/Striking (Greater)|Striking (Greater)]] rune etched into it. This weapon also has an increased damage dice, dealing 1d6 instead of 1d4, 1d8 instead of 1d6, and so on (to a maximum of 1d12).
