# Amulet of Seduction
## Item 6
### [[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 250 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** worn

While this amulet is worn, you gain a +2 item bonus to Deception and Diplomacy checks made to seduce another creature. In addition, any creature within 5 feet of you takes a –1 circumstance penalty to checks against your Deception and Diplomacy DCs.

* * *

**Craft Requirements** The initial raw materials must include one succubus horn.
