# Alicorn Dust
## Item 6
### [[consumable]][[creature-component]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 50 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:2` Interact

* * *

This alicorn dust is made from ground-up unicorn horn. Consuming this dust cures you of any poison affecting you, no counteract check required. If the dust is harvested, you cannot harvest a unicorn horn from the creature.
