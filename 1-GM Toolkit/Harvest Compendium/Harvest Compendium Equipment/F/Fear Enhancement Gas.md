# Fear Enhancement Gas
## Item 3
### [[consumable]][[magical]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 9 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:0` manipulate

**Trigger** You cause another creature to become frightened.

* * *

You activate this gas by inhaling it. When you do, the value of the frightened condition you're imposing on the target increases by 1, to a maximum of 3.

* * *

**Craft Requirements** The initial crafting materials must include one vial of fear pheromones.
