# Nilith Claws
## Item 2
### [[creature-component]]


**Price** 15 gp; 
**Bulk** 1; **Size** med
**Usage** other

This claw has a nasty curve and a sharp point. A claw can be used to craft a weapon from the knife weapon group. This weapon has an increased damage dice, dealing 1d6 damage instead of 1d4, 1d8 instead of 1d6, and so on (to a maximum of 1d12). It also has the agile weapon trait if it doesn't already.
