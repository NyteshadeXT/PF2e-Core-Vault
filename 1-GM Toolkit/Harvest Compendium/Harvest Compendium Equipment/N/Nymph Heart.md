# Nymph Heart
## Item 3
### [[consumable]][[creature-component]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 10 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:0` manipulate

**Trigger** You cast a spell with the emotion trait that targets another creature and requires them to make a saving throw.

* * *

The heart looks like a bundle of roots or watery flowers, depending on the type of nymph it was harvested from. When you activate it, the creature targeted by the spell suffers a -1 circumstance penalty on its save.
