# Quelaunt's Limbs
## Item 8
### [[creature-component]]


**Price** 15 gp; 
**Bulk** 1; **Size** med
**Usage** other

These limbs are covered in pulsing veins, each ending in three sharp claws. A limb can be used to craft a weapon from the club weapon group. This weapon has an increased damage dice, dealing 1d6 damage instead of 1d4, 1d8 damage instead of 1d6, and so on (to a maximum of 1d12). It also has the grapple and shove weapon traits if it doesn't already.
