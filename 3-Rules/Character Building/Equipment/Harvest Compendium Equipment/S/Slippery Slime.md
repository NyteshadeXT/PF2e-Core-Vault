**Activate** `pf2:1` Strike

* * *

This slime is sticky and oily to the touch. On a hit, a struck creature becomes [[Conditions/Clumsy|Clumsy 1]] and takes a -5-foot penalty to its Speeds for 1 hour or until the slime is removed. The slime can be removed with a total of three Interact actios by the entangled creature or creatures adjacent to the creature. These actions don't need to be consecutive or made by the same creature.
