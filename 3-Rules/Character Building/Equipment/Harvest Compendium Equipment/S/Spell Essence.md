# Spell Essence
## Item 1
### [[consumable]][[magical]][[talisman]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 3 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** affixed-to-armor

**Activate** `pf2:0` manipulate

**Trigger** You cast a spell that requires you to roll damage

**Requirements** You are trained in Arcana.

* * *

This iridescent liquid produces a thick mist and sharp smell. When you activate it and roll damage for the spell, you can reroll any results of 1 or 2 and must take the new results.

* * *

**Craft Requirements** Expend one spell slot.
