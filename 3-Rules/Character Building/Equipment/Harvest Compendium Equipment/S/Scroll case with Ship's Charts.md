# Scroll case with Ship's Charts

### 


**Price** 1 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

If you spend 1 hour studying the charts in this case, you gain a further understanding of the configuration of the nearby shoreline and seafloor. You gain a +2 item bonus to checks to steer and pilot ships and boats in the described area. This bonus lasts for a number of hours equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of 1).
