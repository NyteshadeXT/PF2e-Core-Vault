# Shemhazian Tail
## Item 14
### [[creature-component]]


**Price** 450 gp; 
**Bulk** 1; **Size** med
**Usage** other

This tail is long, heavy, and surprisingly balanced. The tail can be used to craft a weapon from the spear or polearm weapon groups. The weapon has a [[Equipment/Striking (Greater)|Striking (Greater)]] rune etched into it. The weapon also has the following feature.

* * *

**Activate** `pf2:r` manipulate

**Trigger** A creature within reach of your weapon leaves a square during a move action it's taking

* * *

**Effect** You attempt to [[Actions/Trip|Trip]] the creature.
