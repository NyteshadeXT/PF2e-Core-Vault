# Rakshasa Hand
## Item 7
### [[consumable]][[creature-component]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 55 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:2` Interact

* * *

This hand is affixed in reverse to its wrist. When you activate the hand, you break a finger and cast a 3rd-rank [[Spells/Mind Reading|Mind Reading]] spell, using your spellcasting ability and without expending a spell slot. You must be an occult spellcaster to use the component in this way and have a spell slot of the appropriate rank, but you do not need to know the spell or have it prepared. A hand has 1d4 unbroken fingers when harvested.
