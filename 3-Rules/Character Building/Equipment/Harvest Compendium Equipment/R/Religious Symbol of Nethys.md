# Religious Symbol of Nethys

### 


**Price** 1 sp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

If you're brandishing this symbol, you gain a +1 item bonus to Deception checks made to present yourself as a follower of Nethys.
