# Nightmare Smoke
## Item 5
### [[consumable]][[magical]][[poison]][[talisman]]

**Category** talisman; 
**Price** 30 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** affixed-to-armor

**Activate** `pf2:r` Interact

**Trigger** You take damage from a hostile source.

* * *

This smoke is thick and black. When you take damage, you can break the vial and unleash the smoke. The smoke fills a 15-foot radius burst centered on one corner of your space. All creatures within that area are [[Conditions/Concealed|Concealed]], and all other creatures are concealed to them. Any creature that starts its turn within the smoke becomes [[Conditions/Sickened|Sickened 2]] (`DC 19 Fortitude check` negates). The smoke lasts for 1 minute or until dispersed by a strong wind. Nightmares, nightmare riders, creatures that aren’t breathing or don’t breathe, and creatures immune to poison are unaffected to the aura’s sickened effect but not the concealment
