# Nightmare Fuel
## Item 6
### [[ingredient]]


**Price** 35 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** other

The contents of this vial are more gas than liquid and fill you with a sense of impending doom. Nightmare fuel can be used to craft a dose of [[Equipment/Slumber Wine|Slumber Wine]], a dose of [[Unsweet Dreams|Unsweet Dreams]], or a [[Nightmare Smoke|Nightmare Smoke]].
