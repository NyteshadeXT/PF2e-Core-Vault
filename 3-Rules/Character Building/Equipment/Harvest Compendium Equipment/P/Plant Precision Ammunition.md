# Plant Precision Ammunition
## Item 2
### [[consumable]][[magical]]


**Price** 5 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** carried

**Ammunition** arrow, bolt

* * *

A creature hit with this ammunition takes 1d4 precision damage. This bonus increases to 2d4 if the creature is a plant.

* * *

**Craft Requirements** The initial crafting materials must include one flytrap tooth or other suitable tooth (see [[Harvest Compendium Journals/2. Bestiary Creature Entries|Bestiary Creature Entries]])
