# Pure Light
## Item 2
### [[ingredient]]


**Price** 3 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

This vial of liquid glows, shedding bright light in a 10-foot radius and dim light for the next 10 feet. It can be used to craft a [[Equipment/Candle of Revealing|Candle of Revealing]], a [[Equipment/Candle of Truth|Candle of Truth]], [[Equipment/Ghost Ink|Ghost Ink]], or a piece of [[Equipment/Shining Ammunition|Shining Ammunition]].
