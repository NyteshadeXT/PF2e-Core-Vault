# Pure Flame Essence
## Item 10
### [[consumable]][[creature-component]][[talisman]]

**Category** talisman; 
**Price** 200 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** affixed-to-armor

**Activate** `pf2:0` manipulate

**Trigger** You cast a spell that requires you to roll for fire damage

**Requirements** You have fire resistance 5 or higher.

* * *

This essence looks and feels like ash, but if sprinkled over a flammable source it instantly catches flame. When you activate it, instead of rolling damage for the spell, you deal the maximum possible amount of damage.
