# Pure Frost Crystals
## Item 10
### [[consumable]][[creature-component]][[talisman]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 200 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:0` manipulate

**Trigger** You cast a spell that requires you to roll for cold damage

**Requirements** You have cold resistance 5 or higher.

* * *

This essence looks exactly like pure ice but it never melts. A cold mist steams from the shards. When you activate it, instead of rolling damage for the spell, you deal the maximum possible amount of damage.
