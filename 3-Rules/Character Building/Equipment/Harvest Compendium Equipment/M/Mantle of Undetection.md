# Mantle of Undetection
## Item 6
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 150 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** worn

This mantle allows its wearer to remain undetectable.

* * *

**Activate** `pf2:r` envision

**Frequency** once per hour

**Trigger** A detection, revelation, or scrying divination attempts to target or learn about you

**Effect** You try to counteract the effect using your Stealth modifier as your counteract modifier.
