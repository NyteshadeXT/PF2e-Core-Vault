# Wendigo Eyes
## Item 6
### [[consumable]][[creature-component]][[edible]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 50 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:1` Interact

This eye has no pupil and reflects light. After consuming this eye, you gain darkvision for 1 hour. For the same duration, you have heatsight out of a range of 60 feet. Heatsight is a precise sense that sees heat signatures.
