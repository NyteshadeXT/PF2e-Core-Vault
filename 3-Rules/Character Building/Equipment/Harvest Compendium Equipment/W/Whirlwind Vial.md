# Whirlwind Vial
## Item 5
### [[consumable]][[magical]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 25 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:2` Interact

* * *

When you activate this vial, you open it and let out a storm of elemental energy, whipping up a whirlwind around you. The area within a 20-foot emanation from you is difficult terrain for Striding and Flying creatures for 1 minute. Creatures with the air trait are unaffected.
