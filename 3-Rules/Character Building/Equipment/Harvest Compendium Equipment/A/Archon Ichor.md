# Archon Ichor
## Item 4
### [[consumable]][[creature-component]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 13 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:0` manipulate

**Trigger** You cast [[Spells/Translocate|Translocate]] and you have seen another creature cast _Translocate_ within the last round.

* * *

This ichor looks like blood but it has no smell. When you activate the ichor, assuming your _Translocate_ has enough distance, you appear the same distance and direction from the creature as before either of you used _Translocate._
