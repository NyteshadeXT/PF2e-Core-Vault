This staff is made of white oak and has a polished, whole unicorn horn—also known as alicorn—as its head.

* * *

**Activate** Cast a Spell

**Frequency** once per day

* * *

**Effect** You cast a spell from the staff, targeting a creature that is friendly to you and who is wielding a weapon. Along with the normal effects of the spell, one weapon the creature is wielding of your choice is imbued with the effects of the [[Equipment/Ghost Touch|Ghost Touch]] property rune for 1 hour. A creature wearing [[Equipment/Handwraps of Mighty Blows|Handwraps of Mighty Blows]] can also be targeted with this effect.

**Activate** Cast a Spell

* * *

**Effect** You expend a number of charges from the staff to cast a spell from its list.

*   **Cantrip** [[Spells/Light|Light]]
*   **1st** [[Spells/Detect Poison|Detect Poison]], [[Spells/Heal|Heal]]
*   **2nd** [[Spells/Heal|Heal]]
*   **3rd** [[Spells/Cleanse Affliction]] (poison only)

* * *

**Craft Requirements** Supply one casting of all listed levels of all listed spells, and the initial raw materials must include one unicorn horn
