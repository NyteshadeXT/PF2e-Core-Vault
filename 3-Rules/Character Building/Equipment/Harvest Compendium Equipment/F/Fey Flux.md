# Fey Flux
## Item 1
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[consumable]][[magical]][[potion]]

**Category** potion; 
**Price** 3 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:1` Interact

* * *

The liquid in this vial is kaleidoscopic, changing in color and consistency as the vial turns. Drinking the flux causes chaos to enter your body, affecting your future failures and successes. The effects of fey flux last for 24 hours or until triggered, after which the flux leaves your body. A creature can only benefit from one dose of fey flux at a time, and the flux cannot be purposefully triggered, such as by attacking an ally, enforcing a skill check, or similar. If it is unclear whether the flux would be triggered, the GM determines if it would be triggered or not.

Make a `DC 10 Flat check` when you drink the flux.

* * *

**Success** The first failure or critical failure you roll within the duration turns into a success.

**Failure** The first success or critical success you roll within the duration turns into a failure.
