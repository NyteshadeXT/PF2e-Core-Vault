# Fake Relics

### 


**Price** 1 sp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** carried

These relics look valuable to the untrained eye, increasing in value tenfold. When attempting to sell the relics, the buyer must succeed at a DC 15 Religion check to determine that the relics are fake.
