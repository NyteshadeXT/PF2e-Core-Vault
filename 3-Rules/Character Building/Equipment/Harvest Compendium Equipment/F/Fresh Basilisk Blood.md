# Fresh Basilisk Blood
## Item 1
### [[creature-component]]


**Price** 1 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:2` Interact

* * *

This blood is highly viscous. A vial of basilisk blood can be used to coat a creature that has been turned to stone by a creature's Petrifying Gaze, instantly restoring it to flesh. The blood can only be harvested from a basilisk that has been dead for less than an hour, and it loses its properties 1 hour after it has been harvested.
