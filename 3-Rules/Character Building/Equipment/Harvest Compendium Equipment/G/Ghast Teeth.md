# Ghast Teeth
## Item 3
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[consumable]][[magical]][[talisman]]

**Category** talisman; 
**Price** 10 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** affixed-to-weapon

**Activate** `pf2:0` envision

**Trigger** You successfully Strike a living, non-elf creature with the affixed weapon

* * *

The tooth numbs you if you touch it. When you activate the tooth, the creature must succeed at a `DC 17 Fortitude check` save or become [[Conditions/Paralyzed|Paralyzed]]. It can attempt a new save at the end of each of its turns, and the DC cumulatively decreases by 1 on each such save.
