# Book on Law and Rhetoric

### 


**Price** 3 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

If you consult this book while trying to Recall Knowledge about the laws of the described region, you gain a +1 item bonus to the roll.

In addition, if you spend 1 hour studying the book, you gain an insight into the rhetoric employed among the upper echelons of local society. You gain a +2 item bonus to Charisma-based checks against local nobles and upper-class citizens. This bonus lasts for a number of hours equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of 1).
