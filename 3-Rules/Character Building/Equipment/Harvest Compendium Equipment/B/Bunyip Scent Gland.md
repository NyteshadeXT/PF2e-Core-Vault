# Bunyip Scent Gland
## Item 3
### [[consumable]][[creature-component]][[edible]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 10 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:1` Interact

* * *

This gland is small and oily. After eating this gland, you gain scent as an imprecise scent to a range of 60 feet. You also gain blood scent, which allows you to smell blood in a body of water from up to 1 mile away. These senses last for 1 hour.
