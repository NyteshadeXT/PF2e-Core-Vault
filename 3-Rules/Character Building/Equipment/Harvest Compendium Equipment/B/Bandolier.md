# Bandolier

### 


**Price** 5 sp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** worn

This bandolier is worn over the chest, allowing easy access to certain types of equipment. A bandolier can hold up to six items of light Bulk. Items on the bandolier can be drawn as part of the action that requires them.
