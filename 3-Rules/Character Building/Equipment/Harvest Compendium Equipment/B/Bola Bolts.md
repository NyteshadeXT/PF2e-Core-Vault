# Bola Bolts
## Item 2
### [[consumable]]


**Price** 5 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** carried

**Ammunition** bolt

* * *

This shot deals no damage, but on a hit, the target must succeed `DC 16 Reflex check` save or be knocked [[Conditions/Prone|Prone]] and [[Conditions/Immobilized|Immobilized]] until it [[Actions/escape dc=15|escape dc=15]]{Escapes (DC 15)}. This check can be attempted either by the target or a creature adjacent to the target.
