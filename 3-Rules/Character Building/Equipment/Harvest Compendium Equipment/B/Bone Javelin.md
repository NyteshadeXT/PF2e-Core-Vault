This javelin is made of pure bone and hums with potent magical energy. The weapon is a _+2_ _greater striking_, it deals an additional 2d6 void damage on a successful hit, and it has a range increment of 60 feet instead of the standard 30 feet.

Once it has been used to attack a creature, the bone javelin loses its properties and becomes a normal javelin.
