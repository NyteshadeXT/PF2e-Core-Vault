# Executioner's Hood
## Item 1
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")


**Price** 5 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** worn

This black hood covers most of the face, leaving only gaps for the eyes and mouth. While wearing it, you gain a +1 item bonus to Intimidation checks, and but gain a -2 item penalty to Perception checks that rely on sight.
