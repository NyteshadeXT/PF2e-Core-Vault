# Displacement Cloak
## Item 14
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 2700 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** worncloak

This cloak shifts in space and bends the light, making it harder to hit the wearer. While wearing this cloak, you gain a +2 item bonus to Stealth checks.

* * *

**Activate** `pf2:1` manipulate

**Frequency** once per day

**Effect** For the next 10 minutes, you are harder to hit. Creatures targeting you must attempt a `DC 11 Flat check` check, as if you were [[Conditions/Hidden|Hidden]], even though you remain observed.

Things such as the [[Feats/Blind-Fight|Blind-Fight]] feat and halfling’s [[Ancestry Features/Keen Eyes|Keen Eyes]] that apply to the flat check against hidden creatures also apply against you when you are under the effects of the cloak.

* * *

**Craft Requirements** The initial raw materials must include two sections of astradaemon hide.
