# Cloak of Reactive Resistance
## Item 6
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 250 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** worncloak

This cloak is covered in small dragon scales. While wearing this cloak, you gain a +1 item bonus to AC.

* * *

**Activate** `pf2:r` envision 

**Frequency** once per hour

**Trigger** You take damage matching the type of dragon the wings were harvested from (see the table below)

**Effect** You gain resistance 10 to the triggering damage.

[[Harvest Compendium Effects/Effect_ Cloak of Reactive Resistance|Effect: Cloak of Reactive Resistance]]

* * *

**Craft Requirements** The initial raw materials must include two sections of dragon wing with matching damage type.

  

| Dragon Type | Resistance |
| --- | --- |
| Black or copper | Acid |
| Blue or bronze | Electricity |
| Brass, gold, or red | Fire |
| Green | Poison |
| Silver or white | Cold |
