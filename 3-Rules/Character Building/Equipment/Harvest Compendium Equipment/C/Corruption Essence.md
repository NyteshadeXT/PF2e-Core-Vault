# Corruption Essence
## Item 7
### [[alchemical]][[consumable]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 320 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

This liquid is dark and sour smelling. If poured or thrown into a body of liquid 5 cubic feet or smaller in size, the essence permanently befouls it. The liquid becomes undrinkable and unable to support water-breathing life. This effect destroys water magic or alchemical items if they’re of a lower level than the corruption essence
