# Hooded Cloak

### 


**Price** 1 sp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** worncloak

This cloak has a hood, letting you easier mingle with the crowd. While wearing the cloak, you gain a +1 item bonus to Stealth checks made to [[Actions/Hide|Hide]] or [[Actions/Sneak|Sneak]] in a crowd of people.
