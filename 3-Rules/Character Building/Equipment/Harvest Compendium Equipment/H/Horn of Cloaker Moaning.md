# Horn of Cloaker Moaning
## Item 6
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 250 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

This horn has a shifting purple glow. It can be played as an instrument, granting a +2 item bonus to Performance checks.

**Activate** `pf2:2` manipulate (auditory, emotion, mental);

**Frequency** once per day

**Effect** You blow in the horn and create deep moan, affecting nearby creatures with a random effect. Each creature within a 20-foot emanation that can hear the horn other than you must attempt a DC 20 Will save to resist the horn’s effect.

*   **Fear** (fear, incapacitation). Affected creatures become [[Conditions/Frightened|Frightened 1]] (or, on a critical failure, [[Conditions/Frightened|Frightened 2]] and [[Conditions/Fleeing|Fleeing]] for 1 round). `DC 20 Will check`
*   **Nausea**. Affected creatures fall [[Conditions/Prone|Prone]] and become [[Conditions/Sickened|Sickened 2]]. `DC 20 Will check`

* * *

**Craft Requirements** The initial raw materials must include one set of cloaker vocal cords.
