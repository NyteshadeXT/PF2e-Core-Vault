# Horn of Vrock Screeching
## Item 7
### 


**Price** 350 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

This horn has a black vrock feathers attached to it. It can be played as an instrument, granting a +2 item bonus to Performance checks.

* * *

**Activate** `pf2:2` manipulate (auditory, divine, incapacitation, sonic)

**Frequency** once per day

**Effect** You blow in the horn and create a shrill screech. Each non-demon creature within a 30-foot burst other than you must attempt a `DC 23 Fortitude check` save. On a failure, the creature is [[Conditions/Stunned|Stunned 2]] (or [[Conditions/Stunned|Stunned 3]] on a critical failure)

* * *

**Craft Requirements** The initial raw materials must include one set of vrock vocal cords.
