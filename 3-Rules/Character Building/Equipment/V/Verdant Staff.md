# Verdant Staff
## Item 6
### [[magical]][[3-Rules/Character Building/Equipment/S/Staff]][[two-hand-d8]]

|  |  |
| -- | -- |
| **Category** simple melee weapon | **Group** club |
| **Price** 225 gp |  |
| **Bulk** 1 | **Size** med |
| **Damage** 1d4 bludgeoning  | **Usage** held-in-one-hand |



This oak branch grows leaves in spring that change color in autumn and shed in winter. While wielding it, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to checks to identify plants and fungi.

* * *

**Activate** Cast a Spell

**Effect** You expend a number of charges from the staff to cast a spell from its list.

*   **Cantrips** [[Tangle Vine|Tangle Vine]]
*   **1st** [[Runic Body|Runic Body]], [[Runic Weapon|Runic Weapon]]
*   **2nd** [[Entangling Flora|Entangling Flora]], [[Oaken Resilience|Oaken Resilience]], [[One with Plants|One with Plants]], [[Shape Wood|Shape Wood]]

* * *

**Craft Requirements** Supply one casting of all listed ranks of all listed spells.
