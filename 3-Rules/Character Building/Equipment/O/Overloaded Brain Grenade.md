# Overloaded Brain Grenade
## Item 10
### [unique](unique.md "Unique Rarity Trait")[[alchemical]][[consumable]][[3-Rules/Trait Reference/F/fire]][[3-Rules/Trait Reference/M/mental]][[splash]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 200 gp; 
**Bulk** 1; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-two-hands

**Activate** `pf2:2` Interact

* * *

It's possible for a desperate alchemist to rig the brain container of a defeated alchemical golem to create a volatile (if unusual) explosive. You throw the grenade up to 60 feet, and it explodes, dealing 3d6 fire damage and 3d6 mental damage in a @Template\[burst|distance:10\] (DC 27 Reflex check save).

On a critical failure, a creature takes 2d6 persistent fire damage.

**Craft Requirements** Supply a brain case harvested from alchemical golem
