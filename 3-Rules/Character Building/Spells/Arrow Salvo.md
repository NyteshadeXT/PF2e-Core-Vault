# Arrow Salvo  `pf2:2`  
## Spell 6
### [[concentrate]][[manipulate]][[Wood]]
**Traditions** [[arcane]], [[primal]]
**Range** 100 feet
**Area** 30-foot burst
**Saving Throw** basic reflex
* * * 
You conjure an immense wooden bow that launches a salvo of massive arrows at all nearby enemies. All creatures in the affected area take 8d10 piercing damage, with a basic Reflex save. On a critical failure, the target is knocked [[Conditions/Prone|Prone]] from the force of the impact.

* * *

**Heightened (+1)** Increase the damage by 1d10.
