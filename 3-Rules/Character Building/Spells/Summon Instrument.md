# Summon Instrument  `pf2:3`  
## Cantrip 1
### [[cantrip]][[concentrate]][[manipulate]]
**Traditions** [[arcane]], [[divine]], [[occult]]
**Duration** 1 hour
* * * 
You materialize a [[Equipment/Musical Instrument (Handheld)|Musical Instrument (Handheld)]] in your grasp. The instrument is typical for its type, but it plays only for you. It vanishes when the spell ends. If you cast _summon instrument_ again, any instrument you previously summoned disappears.

* * *

**Heightened (5th)** The instrument is instead a [[Equipment/Musical Instrument (Virtuoso handheld)|Musical Instrument (Virtuoso handheld)]]

*Source: Pathfinder Player Core*
