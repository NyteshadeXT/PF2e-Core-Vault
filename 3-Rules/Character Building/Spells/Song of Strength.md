# Song of Strength  `pf2:1`  
## Cantrip 1
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[bard]][[cantrip]][[composition]][[concentrate]][[emotion]][[mental]]

**Area** 60-foot emanation
**Duration** 1 round
* * * 
You bolster your allies' physical strength with a hearty exhortation. You and your allies gain a +1 status bonus to Athletics checks and to their DCs against Athletics skill actions such as [[Actions/Disarm|Disarm]], [[Actions/Reposition|Reposition]], [[Actions/Shove|Shove]], and [[Actions/Trip|Trip]].
