# Tireless Worker  `pf2:2`  
## Focus 4
### [[cleric]][[concentrate]][[focus]][[healing]][[manipulate]]

**Range** 30 feet
**Targets** 1 creature
**Duration** 1 minute
* * * 
You suppress your choice of one of the following conditions that's affecting the target: [[Conditions/Clumsy 1|Clumsy]], [[Conditions/Encumbered|Encumbered]], [[Conditions/Enfeebled 1|Enfeebled]], or [[Conditions/Fatigued|Fatigued]]. If you don't remove the effect that caused the condition, the condition returns after the spell's duration expires.
