# Void Warp  `pf2:2`  
## Cantrip 1
### [[cantrip]][[concentrate]][[manipulate]][[void]]
**Traditions** [[arcane]], [[divine]], [[occult]]
**Range** 30 feet
**Targets** 1 living creature
**Saving Throw** basic fortitude
* * * 
You call upon the Void to harm life force. The target takes 2d4 void damage with a basic Fortitude save. On a critical failure, the target is also [[Conditions/Enfeebled 1|Enfeebled 1]] until the start of your next turn.

* * *

**Heightened (+1)** The damage increases by 1d4.
