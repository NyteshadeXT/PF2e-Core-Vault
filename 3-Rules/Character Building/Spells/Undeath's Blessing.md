# Undeath's Blessing  `pf2:1`  
## Focus 1
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[focus]][[manipulate]][[sorcerer]][[void]]

**Range** touch
**Targets** 1 living creature touched
**Saving Throw**  will
**Duration** 1 minute
* * * 
You instill within a creature the touch of the grave. For the duration, [[Spells/Harm|Harm]] and [[Spells/Heal|Heal]] spells treat the creature as undead. In addition, harm spells gain a +2 status bonus to the Hit Points restored to the target.

If the target wishes to avoid the spell, it can attempt a Will save to reduce the effects.

* * *

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.

**Success** The target heals half as much from _heal_ and takes half as much damage from _harm_ for 1 round.

**Failure** Effects as described in the text.

* * *

**Heightened (+1)** The status bonus to the Hit Points restored increases by 2.

*Source: Pathfinder Core Rulebook*
