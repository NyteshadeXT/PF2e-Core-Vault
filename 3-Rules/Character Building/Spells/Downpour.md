# Downpour  `pf2:2`  
## Focus 4
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[cleric]][[concentrate]][[focus]][[manipulate]][[water]]

**Range** 120 feet
**Duration** 1 minute
* * * 
You call forth a torrential downpour, which extinguishes nonmagical flames. Creatures in the area are [[Conditions/Concealed|Concealed]] and gain fire resistance 10. Creatures outside the area are Concealed to those inside the area. If a creature with weakness to water ends its turn in the area, the downpour triggers its weakness.  
  

[[Spell Effects/Spell Effect_ Downpour|Spell Effect: Downpour]]

* * *

**Heightened (+1)** The fire resistance increases by 2.
