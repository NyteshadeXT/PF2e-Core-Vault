# Planar Tether  `pf2:2`  
## Spell 4
### [[concentrate]][[manipulate]]
**Traditions** [[arcane]], [[divine]], [[occult]]
**Range** 30 feet
**Targets** 1 creature
**Saving Throw**  will
**Duration** varies
* * * 
You stitch the target to its current plane. While the target is affected by _planar tether_, the spell attempts to counteract any teleportation effect that would move the target, or any effect that would transport it to a different plane. _Planar tether_'s duration is determined by the target's Will save.

* * *

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.

**Success** The effect's duration is 1 minute.

**Failure** The effect's duration is 10 minutes.

**Critical Failure** The effect's duration is 1 hour.
