# Fey Disappearance  `pf2:1`  
## Focus 3
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[focus]][[manipulate]][[sorcerer]]

**Duration** until the end of your next turn
* * * 
You become [[Conditions/Invisible|Invisible]] and ignore natural difficult terrain (such as underbrush). Any hostile action you use ends this invisibility, but you still ignore natural difficult terrain.

* * *

**Heightened (5th)** If you use a hostile action, the invisibility doesn't end.
