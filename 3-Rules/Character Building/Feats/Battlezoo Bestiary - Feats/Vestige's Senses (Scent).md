# Vestige's Senses (Scent)
## Feat 4
### [[archetype]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** you must defeat a monster with scent; Vestige Hunter Dedication
* * *
You incorporated a vestige from a monster with additional senses, granting you those you had not yet gained from your first vestiges. This often takes the form of the monster's snouts placed somewhere in your equipment. You gain imprecise scent with a range of 30 feet.
