# G'mayun Weapon Expertise
## Feat 13
### 

**Category** ancestry; 



**Prerequisites** G’mayun Weapon Familiarity
* * *
You blend your passions for g’mayun weapons and your class’s training and techniques, granting you great skill with g’mayun weapons. Whenever you gain a class feature that grants you expert or greater proficiency in certain weapons, you also gain that proficiency in daggers, falchions, glaives, kukris, scimitars, and all g’mayun weapons in which you are trained.
