# Bounding Reposition `pf2:r`
## Feat 9
### 

**Category** ancestry; 




**Trigger** A foe misses you with a melee attack.

* * *

After dodging your foe's attack, you instinctively hop away from further danger. You [[Actions/Leap|Leap]]. If you have [[Feats/Unbelievable Leaper|Unbelievable Leaper]], you can [[Actions/High Jump|High Jump]] or [[Actions/Long Jump|Long Jump]] instead, but you can't Stride as part of these actions.
