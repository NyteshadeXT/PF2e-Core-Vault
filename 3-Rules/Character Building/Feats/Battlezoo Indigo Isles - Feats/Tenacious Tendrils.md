# Tenacious Tendrils
## Feat 13
### 

**Category** ancestry; 



**Prerequisites** Tendril Trap
* * *
Your tenacity allows you to create more powerful tendril traps more quickly. You can use Tendril Trap once per hour, instead of once per day, and creatures take 4 poison damage per square from your Tendril Trap, instead of 2 poison Damage.
