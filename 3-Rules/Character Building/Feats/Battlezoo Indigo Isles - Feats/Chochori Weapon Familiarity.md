# Chochori Weapon Familiarity
## Feat 1
### 

**Category** ancestry; 




You know how to fight with a variety of aquadynamic piercing weapons. You are trained with daggers, halberds, lances, longspears, rapiers, spears, tridents. In addition, you gain access to all uncommon chochori weapons. For the purpose of determining your proficiency, martial chochori weapons are simple weapons and advanced chochori weapons are martial weapons.
