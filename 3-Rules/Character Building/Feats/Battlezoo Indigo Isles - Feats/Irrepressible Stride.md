# Irrepressible Stride `pf2:2`
## Feat 13
### 

**Category** ancestry; 




**Frequency** once per hour

* * *

Nothing can keep you from traveling. You Stride and then Step, or Step and then Stride, ignoring difficult terrain, greater difficult terrain, and hazardous terrain during this movement. If an effect prevents you from moving, you can attempt to [[Actions/Escape|Escape]] the effect first, taking the other actions if you succeed.
