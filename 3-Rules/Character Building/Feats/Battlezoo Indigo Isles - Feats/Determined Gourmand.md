# Determined Gourmand
## Feat 1
### 

**Category** ancestry; 




In sickness or in health, you vow to eat as much as you can, regardless of your stomach’s rumbling or the protestations of your body. The sickened condition doesn’t prevent you from ingesting anything. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Fortitude check saves made to reduce the sickened condition by retching.
