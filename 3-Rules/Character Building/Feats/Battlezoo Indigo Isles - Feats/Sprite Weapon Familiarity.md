# Sprite Weapon Familiarity
## Feat 1
### [[3-Rules/Bestiaries/Monster Core/S/Sprite]]

**Category** ancestry; 




You favor bows, dextrous blades, and the weapons of the fey. You are trained with longbows, composite longbows, rapiers, shortbows, composite shortbows, and shortswords.

In addition, you gain access to all uncommon sprite weapons. For the purpose of determining your proficiency, martial sprite weapons are simple weapons and advanced sprite weapons are martial weapons.
