# Vigilant Sentry
## Feat 17
### [[fortune]]

**Category** ancestry; 



**Prerequisites** Uncanny Vigilance
* * *
You signal your allies when danger is near, and your vigilance improves their odds of survival. When you use Uncanny Vigilance, any creatures you choose within 30 feet that are attempting a Reflex saving throw against the same effect (such as a single spell) or are rolling initiative for the same encounter gain a +2 circumstance bonus on their save or roll.
