# Push Past Pain `pf2:1`
## Feat 9
### 

**Category** ancestry; 




**Frequency** once per day

**Requirements** You have the [[Conditions/Wounded|Wounded]] condition.

* * *

When injured, you grit your teeth and force your body to heal by the strength of your will alone. For the next 4 rounds, you gain fast healing equal to half your level, plus twice the value of your wounded condition (for example, fast healing 10 if you are 9th level and wounded 3).
