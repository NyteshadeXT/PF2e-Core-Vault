# Empathetic Aura `pf2:1`
## Feat 13
### [[aura]][[3-Rules/Trait Reference/M/mental]]

**Category** ancestry; 



**Prerequisites** rhizo heritage
* * *
**Frequency** once per day

You exude an aura of understanding for 1 minute. You and all allies within 15 feet gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saving throws against mental effects. This increases to a +2 circumstance bonus if you're a master in Diplomacy and a +3 circumstance bonus if you're legendary.
