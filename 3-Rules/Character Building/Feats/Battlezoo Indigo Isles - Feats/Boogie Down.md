# Boogie Down
## Feat 8
### [[archetype]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** Rumplank Reveler Dedication; master in Performance
* * *
Your performance is so catchy that those around you can't help but want to follow along with your choreography. You Stride in one direction. If your Stride is successful, all creatures affected by [[Actions/Revel|Revel]] get +10-foot status bonus to their Speed for 1 round as long as they move in the same direction that you moved.
