# Keep This Party Going
## Feat 4
### [[archetype]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** Rumplank Reveler Dedication
* * *
**Requirements** You are benefiting from [[Actions/Revel|Revel]]

* * *

You know how to keep a party going by passing out extra food, pouring your best drinks, or simply kicking things into high gear with everyone's favorite song. Attempt a check in the skill chosen for your Revel. The DC is usually a standard-difficulty DC of a level equal to the highest-level character benefiting from your Revel, but the GM can assign a different DC based on the circumstances.

* * *

**Critical Success** The Revel lasts until the end of your turn after next. Until the beginning of your next turn, the status bonus to saving throws increases to +2.

**Success** The Revel lasts until the end of your next turn, or its current duration, whichever is longer.

**Critical Failure** The Revel ends immediately.
