# Coral's Voice, Nature's Ears
## Feat 5
### 

**Category** ancestry; 



**Prerequisites** Speak with Coral
* * *
You branch out from being able to communicate with coral to all other kinds of animals. You can speak to all animals, not just animals that are coral. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to [[Actions/Make an Impression|Make an Impression]] on animals (which usually uses the Diplomacy skill).
