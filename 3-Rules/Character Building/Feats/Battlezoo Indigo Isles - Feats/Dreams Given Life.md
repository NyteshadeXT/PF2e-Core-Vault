# Dreams Given Life `pf2:0`
## Feat 13
### 

**Category** ancestry; 




**Frequency** once per 10 minutes

* * *

If your next action is to cast a conjuration spell that summons a creature with the minion trait, you conjure the summoned creature from your dreams, adapting its form to better suit your needs. The summoned creature gains the dream trait. Additionally, the summoned creature gains one of the following abilities of your choice (or two abilities if you're legendary in Occultism).

*   It gains a climb, fly, or swim speed of 20 feet.
*   It gains darkvision.
*   It gains scent (imprecise, 30 feet).
*   Its Speed increases by 10 feet.
*   It doesn't need to breathe.
*   It can speak and understand one language that you know.
