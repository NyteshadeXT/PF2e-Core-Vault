# Sugar Rush
## Feat 13
### 

**Category** ancestry; 



**Prerequisites** Going Bananas
* * *
You can gain benefits from eating bananas more often, and when the sugar rush goes to your head, you move faster too. You can use [[Feats/Going Bananas|Going Bananas]] once per hour instead of once per day. You gain a +20-foot status bonus to your Speeds as long as you have temporary Hit Points from Going Bananas.
