# Protective Squirt
## Feat 12
### [[archetype]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** Sweat Toxin
* * *
You've learned how to use your poisonous sweat to protect your allies, as well as to protect yourself from ranged attacks. You can use your [[Feats/Sweat Toxin|Sweat Toxin]] reaction if a foe within 15 feet of you hits you or an ally within 15 feet of you with an attack (whether or not it's a melee attack), in addition to its normal trigger.
