# Stubborn Demeanor
## Feat 1
### 

**Category** ancestry; 




You refuse to back down, even when faced with obvious danger. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saving throws against fear effects and a +2 circumstance bonus to your Will DC against Intimidation skill actions, such as attempts to [[Actions/Demoralize|Demoralize]] you.
