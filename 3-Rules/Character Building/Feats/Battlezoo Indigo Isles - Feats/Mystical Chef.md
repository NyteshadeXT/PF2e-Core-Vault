# Mystical Chef
## Feat 9
### 

**Category** ancestry; 




Your ability to conjure and season food is downright magical. You can cast _[[Spells/Create Food|Create Food]]_ and _[[Spells/Enhance Victuals|Enhance Victuals]]_ each once per day as 2nd-level primal innate spells. At 13th level, _enhance victuals_ is heightened to 3rd level. At 17th level, both spells are heightened to 4th level.
