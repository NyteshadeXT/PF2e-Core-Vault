# G'mayun Weapon Passion
## Feat 5
### 

**Category** ancestry; 



**Prerequisites** G’mayun Weapon Familiarity
* * *
Your passion for traditional g’mayun weapons allows you to gain their most deadly benefits when you have your foe at your mercy. Whenever you critically hit using a g’mayun weapon or one of the weapons listed in G’mayun Weapon Familiarity feat, you apply the weapon’s critical specialization effect.
