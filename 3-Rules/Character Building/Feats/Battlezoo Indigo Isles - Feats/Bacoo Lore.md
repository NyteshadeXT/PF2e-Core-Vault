# Bacoo Lore
## Feat 1
### 

**Category** ancestry; 




You know bacoo philosophy about the strength of community, the importance of being cautious amidst the cruelty of the Material Plane, and the ways bacoo defy the gods by encouraging mortals to work together. You become trained in Society and Stealth. If you would automatically become trained in one of those skills (from your background or class, for example), you instead become trained in a skill of your choice. You also become trained in Bacoo Lore.
