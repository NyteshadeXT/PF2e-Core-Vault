# Hardriggan Weapon Familiarity
## Feat 1
### 

**Category** ancestry; 




Hardriggans like to use big, intimidating weapons, and you have learned to use them well. You are trained with falchions, greataxes, greatswords, and war flails.

In addition, you gain access to all uncommon hardriggan weapons. For the purpose of determining your proficiency, martial hardriggan weapons are simple weapons and advanced hardriggan weapons are martial weapons.
