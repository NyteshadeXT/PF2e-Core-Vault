# Persistent Toxin
## Feat 4
### [[archetype]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** Toxicant Sentinel Dedication
* * *
You can make your toxin linger in your foe's system. When you use [[Actions/Exude Toxin|Exude Toxin]], you can choose to deal persistent poison or acid damage (whichever would deal more damage) instead of normal additional damage.
