# Sudden Mutation `pf2:1`
## Feat 1
### 

**Category** ancestry; 




**Frequency** once per day

* * *

Your body possesses unnatural mutability. When you take this feat, select a single common 1st-level mutagen. When you activate this feat, you gain the benefits and drawbacks of that mutagen for 1 minute.

* * *

**Special** You may take this feat more than once, selecting a different mutagen each time.
