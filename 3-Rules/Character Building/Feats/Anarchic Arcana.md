8.  [[3-Rules/Character Building/Spells/S/See the Unseen|See the Unseen]];

10.  [[Noise Blast|Noise Blast]];
11.  [[Gecko Grip|Gecko Grip]];
12.  [[Telekinetic Maneuver|Telekinetic Maneuver]].
