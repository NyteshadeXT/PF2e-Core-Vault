# Prismatic Contamination *Hazard 5*  
[environmental](environmental.md "Environmental Hazard Trait") [magical](3-Rules/trait_reference/M/magical.md "Magical Item Trait") [occult](3-Rules/trait_reference/O/occult.md "Occult Item Trait")  

**Stealth** DC 23 (trained)  
**Description** A disastrously exposed campsite allows unstable crystal resonance, tainted pollen, luminous spores, and impossible echoes from beneath the Canopy to seep into the bodies and dreams of resting creatures.

---
**Disable** DC 23 [[Skills#Survival|Survival]] to relocate the camp and insulate it from resonant crystal growths; DC 23 [[Skills#Nature|Nature]] to identify and avoid contaminated flora, spores, and groundwater; or DC 25 [[Skills#Occultism|Occultism]] to disrupt the lingering resonance before it settles into the sleepers.

**Resonant Corruption** `pf2:r` **Trigger** The PCs complete an 8-hour rest in a disastrously exposed campsite within a contaminated region of the Prismatic Canopy. **Effect** Each exposed creature must attempt a DC 20 Fortitude save.

- **Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.
- **Success** The creature becomes [[sickened|Sickened 1]].
- **Failure** The creature becomes [[drained|Drained 1]] and [[sickened|Sickened 1]].
- **Critical Failure** The creature becomes [[drained|Drained 2]] and [[sickened|Sickened 2]]. The creature also experiences disturbing dreams filled with impossible geometry, crystalline roots, and distant voices beneath the earth.

---
**Reset** Prismatic Contamination can occur each time the PCs rest in a disastrously exposed campsite within a contaminated, unstable, or Dungeon-influenced region of the Prismatic Canopy.
