# Canopy Exposure *Hazard 4*  
[environmental](environmental.md "Environmental Hazard Trait") [disease](disease.md "Disease Hazard Trait")  

**Stealth** DC 22 (trained)  
**Description** Poor shelter, clinging humidity, biting insects, tainted water, and the strange resonant pollen of the Prismatic Canopy expose resting creatures to lingering sickness and exhaustion.

---
**Disable** DC 21 [[Skills#Survival|Survival]] to establish a dry, sheltered, and properly screened campsite; DC 21 [[Skills#Nature|Nature]] to identify and avoid unsafe plants, stagnant water, and insect-breeding ground; or DC 23 [[Skills#Medicine|Medicine]] to treat the party’s exposure before symptoms take hold.

**Canopy Sickness** `pf2:r` **Trigger** The PCs complete an 8-hour rest in a poorly prepared campsite in the Prismatic Canopy. **Effect** Each exposed creature must attempt a DC 18 Fortitude save.

- **Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.
- **Success** The creature is unaffected.
- **Failure** The creature becomes [[drained|Drained 1]] until it receives a full night’s rest in a safe settlement, sanctuary, or properly secured campsite.
- **Critical Failure** The creature becomes [[drained|Drained 1]] and [[fatigued|Fatigued]].

---
**Reset** Canopy Exposure can occur each time the PCs rest in an unsafe or poorly prepared campsite within the Prismatic Canopy.
