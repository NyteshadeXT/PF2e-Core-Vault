# Aura: Empathetic Aura
## Level 1
### 






You and all allies within 15 feet gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saving throws against mental effects. This increases to a +2 circumstance bonus if you're a master in Diplomacy and a +3 circumstance bonus if you're legendary.
