# Effect: Fire Amplification
## Level 1
### 






Creatures within 30 feet of a furnace newt gain a +1 status bonus to damage rolls whenever they deal fire damage. This status bonus increases depending on the furnace newt's increasing size from All-Consuming Fire, to +2 if the newt is Medium, +3 if the newt is Large, and +4 if the newt is Huge.
