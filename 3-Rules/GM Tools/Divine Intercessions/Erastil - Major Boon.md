# Erastil - Major Boon

### 

**Category** deityboon; 




You become a friend to the land.

You can cast [[Speak with Plants|Speak with Plants]] as a divine innate spell.

When you are in a healthy natural environment, the land sustains you without need for food or drink.

You can cast the [[Commune|Commune]] ritual without any secondary casters or secondary checks.

*Source: Pathfinder Lost Omens: Gods & Magic*
