# Brace  
*Source: Treasure Vault p. 24*  

A brace weapon is effective at damaging moving opponents. When you [[3-Rules/Character Building/Actions/Ready|Ready]] to Strike an opponent that moves within your reach, until the start of your next turn Strikes with the brace weapon deal an additional 2 precision damage for each weapon damage die it has.
