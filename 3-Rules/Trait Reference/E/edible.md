# Edible  
*Source: Harvest Compendium p. 10*  

Edible items, like raw meat or an angel’s divine tongue, must be cooked or cured before they can be safely consumed. If you try to consume an edible item that has not been cooked or cured, you must succeed at a standard DC Fortitude save for your level or become sickened 1 for 1 hour. You can cure edible items using the [[Cure Food]] trained action.
