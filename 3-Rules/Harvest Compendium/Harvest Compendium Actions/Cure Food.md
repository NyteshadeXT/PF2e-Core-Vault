```pf2e-stats
### Cure Food

==exploration== ==manipulate==

**Category** interaction; 
**Requirements** You are holding or wearing cookware.

---

You spend 1 hour curing a fresh edible item, such as the heart of a great boar or the tongue of an angel, or up to 10 Bulk of raw food, such as meat taken from a slain animal. For an item to be considered fresh, it must be less than 8 hours since it was harvested (though spells like gentle repose can extend this duration). You must have a fire available for the duration of the curing process.

---

Roll a [[skills#Survival|Survival]] check. The GM sets the DC, which is usually a standard DC for the item’s level.

**Success** The item is cured and has an expiration date of 1 year.
**Failure** The item is ruined and cannot be used.

```
