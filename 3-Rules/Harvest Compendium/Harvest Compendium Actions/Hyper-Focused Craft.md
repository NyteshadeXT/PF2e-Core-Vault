```pf2e-stats
### Hyper-Focused Craft

==exploration== ==manipulate==

**Category** interaction; 

---

You spend 1 hour making headway on a project to [[Craft]] an item or items. During this hour, you focus entirely on your work and can’t do anything else. If you are interrupted during this time, you automatically fail the action.

This action is an extension of the Craft trained action, and it follows the same requirements. At the end of the hour, you make a standard DC [[Skills#Crafting|Crafting]] check for the level of the item you’re trying to make.

---

**Success** You make progress on your project equal to 1 day of work. You are [[Fatigued]]. You cannot use any exploration actions while fatigued in this way.
**Failure** You make no progress on your project, but you don’t waste any materials. You are fatigued. You cannot use any exploration actions while fatigued in this way.

```
