```pf2e-stats
### Process Item

==exploration== ==manipulate==

**Requirements** You are holding or wearing a set of alchemist’s tools.

---

You spend 1 hour refining a venom, poison, or similar item harvested from a creature, thereby removing the unprocessed trait from the item and making it useable.

Roll a [[Skills#Crafting|Crafting]] check. The GM sets the DC, which is usually a standard DC for the item’s level.

---

**Success** The item’s unprocessed trait is removed, and it is now useable.
**Failure** The item is ruined and cannot be used.

```
