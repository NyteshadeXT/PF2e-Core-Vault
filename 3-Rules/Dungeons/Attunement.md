The Dungeon’s attunement reflects the metaphysical principles shaping it.  It represents the kinds of Essences the Dungeon amplifies.  The creatures/resources it generates are guided by the its attunement.  Attunement even direct what powers mutate or strengthen within the Dungeon.

The Dungeon Seed expresses itself in accordance with the Dungeons attunement.

Dungeon Attunements use Essence categories directly. Every dungeon has a **Primary Attunement**. This is the dominant defining resonance.  It can also have multiple **Secondary Attunements** representing other supporting influences which, while not dominant, can be found throughout the Dungeon. **Hidden Attunements** are not found on the Dungeon archway and can be unexpected and dangerous to the unprepared.

# Proposed Dungeon Attunement Rules

## Most Dungeons
- 1 Primary Attunement
- 1–3 Secondary Attunements

High and Cataclysm Dungeons may shift attunements, contain conflicting attunements, have region-based attunements or possess unstable resonance overlaps.

# What Attunements Actually Do
This is where the system gets powerful. Dungeon Attunement can influence:

|System|Possible Effect|
|---|---|
|Creature ecology|What monsters emerge|
|Resources|What materials grow|
|Resonance|Environmental rules|
|Powers|Essence interactions|
|Mutation|Chimeric traits|
|Hazards|Dungeon mechanics|
|Seed behavior|Dungeon goals|
|Exploration|Traversal style|

## Example: Growth Dungeon

**Primary Attunement:**
- Growth

**Secondary Attunements:**
- Fungus
- Hunt

Possible effects:
- regeneration enhanced
- flora aggressive
- healing unstable
- creatures adapt rapidly
- organic materials valuable

# Suggested Attunement Frequency

## Common Dungeon Attunements
Most naturally occurring Dungeons.

Examples:
- Fire
- Water
- Stone
- Hunt
- Growth
- Fungus
- Swift
- Trap
- Wind
- Shadow

## Rare Dungeon Attunements
More dangerous or unusual.

Examples:
- Dominion
- Reflection
- Revelation
- Freedom
- Equilibrium
- Momentum
- Oath
- Rune
- Sound

## Forbidden Attunements
Usually terrifying. These should feel deeply wrong.

Examples:
- Oblivion
- Devouring
- Subjugation
- Paradox
- Ruin
- Entropy
