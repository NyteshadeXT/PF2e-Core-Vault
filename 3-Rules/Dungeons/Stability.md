
# Stability System
Stability is actually more important to adventurers than Rank in day-to-day life. A Stable Greater Dungeon may be safer to explore than a Volatile Lesser Dungeon. 

## Stable
The Dungeon behaves predictably. Stable Dungeons often become "farmed” Dungeons, guild training grounds and/or economic assets. These become economically important “working Dungeons.”

### Characteristics
- Routes mostly consistent
- Resonance fluctuations minimal
- Regeneration understood
- Reliable resource extraction
- Safe expedition infrastructure possible

---

## Shifting
The Dungeon changes regularly but not catastrophically. This is the most common “natural” Dungeon state.

### Characteristics
- Layout alterations common
- Biomes slowly migrate
- Encounter patterns evolve
- Hidden regions emerge periodically
- Mapping remains difficult

---

## Volatile
The Dungeon undergoes dangerous instability events. Volatile Dungeons are lucrative but are often deadly.

### Characteristics
- Resonance surges
- sudden biome transformation
- creature mutation spikes
- environmental disasters
- unstable traversal routes

---

## Breached
Dungeon influence is leaking heavily into the surrounding world.. A Breached Dungeon may trigger quarantines, military intervention, or mass evacuations.

### Characteristics
- Surface corruption
- escaped creatures
- warped ecosystems
- resonance anomalies nearby
- nearby settlements threatened

---

## Awakening
The Seed is becoming more active or evolving. This should feel deeply ominous.

### Characteristics
- New layers and/or regions forming rapidly
- apex activity increasing
- Dungeon intelligence emerging
- internal adaptation accelerating
- resonance becoming aggressive

---

## Collapsing
The Dungeon is dying or destabilizing irreversibly. These become extremely dangerous exploration opportunities. It might result in evacuation scenarios, reality tears or even extinction events. This can happen because of Seed removal or death.  It can also be caused by some form of internal corruption.
 
### Characteristics
- regions disintegrating
- unstable exits
- ecology breakdown
- resonance failure
- catastrophic hazards

---

## Consumed
The Dungeon has expanded beyond containment. This is extremely rare and terrifying.

### Characteristics
- surrounding land assimilated
- Dungeon biome dominates region
- no clear entrance remains
- natural laws and reality permanently altered

---

## Dormant
The Dungeon remains present but largely inactive. A Dormant Dungeon may later reactivate. This is very different from “destroyed.”

### Characteristics
- Low regeneration
- weak resonance
- minimal creature activity
- reduced resource production
- inactive pathways

---

## Cleansed
While exceptionally rare, a Cleansed Dungeon has been neutralized safely. Most people may think this is impossible.

### Characteristics
- resonance dissipated
- creatures gone
- Seed purified or stabilized
- environment recovering
