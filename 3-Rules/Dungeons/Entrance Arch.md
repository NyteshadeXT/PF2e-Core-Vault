The **archway** is the Dungeon’s player-facing “character sheet.” Not exact stats, but readable warnings.

# Dungeon Archway Information
The portal archway could show several rune categories allowing the players to get a sense of what may lie beyond:
- [[Attunement]] what themes shape the Dungeon; both primary and secondary.
- [[Rank]] metaphysical potency, not size or encounter level
- [[Stability]] giving an indication of predictability with in the Dungeon.
- Warning Runes — major dangers falling outside of the normal attunement themes

# Warning Runes
These are the “practical danger” runes. These don’t typically map one-to-one with Essences and represent the hazards and other dangers that might be encountered not inline with the Attunement of the Dungeon.

Some examples include:
- Poison
- Disease
- Fire Hazard
- Psychic Pressure
- Regeneration
- Forced Movement
- Corruption
- Predation
- Swarm
- Darkness
- Flooding
- Verticality
- Temporal Distortion
- Spatial Distortion
- Unstable Magic
- Traps
- Constructs
- Undead
- Divine Influence
- Forbidden Resonance

# Reading the Archway
Reading a Dungeon archway is a Victory Point Challenge representing the party studying the resonance patterns, runes, and metaphysical structure surrounding the Dungeon’s entrance. The information revealed depends on how deeply the party successfully interprets the archway before the Dungeon’s resonance obscures further understanding.

Every Dungeon archway displays a visible arrangement of runes conveying its dominant nature. Experienced adventurers learn to interpret these signs before entering, using them to prepare supplies, equipment, powers, and expedition plans.

The archway does not reveal complete truths. It communicates dominant resonance patterns and practical warnings, but hidden influences, concealed attunements, and distorted meanings may lie beneath the visible structure.

## Victory Point Thresholds

|Victory Points|Understanding Gained|
|---|---|
|2 VP|Basic Understanding|
|4 VP|Detailed Understanding|
|6 VP|Deep Understanding|
|10 VP|Hidden Understanding|

The party may continue attempting checks after reaching a threshold in order to gain deeper insight, but the challenge immediately ends upon reaching 3 Failures.

After 3 Failures, the Dungeon’s resonance interferes with interpretation. No further understanding can be gained and some conclusions reached during the challenge may be incomplete, distorted, or dangerously misleading. The GM should not explicitly inform the players that they have failed.

## Basic Understanding (2 VP)
The party correctly identifies:

- Primary Attunement runes
- Dungeon Rank rune
- Stability rune

This provides a foundational understanding of the Dungeon’s dominant resonance and overall behavior.

## Detailed Understanding (4 VP)
The party additionally identifies:

- Secondary Attunement runes
- Common Warning Runes
- Broad environmental expectations
- Common creature or hazard patterns

This level of understanding reflects competent expedition preparation and is considered sufficient by most professional adventurers.

## Deep Understanding (6 VP)
The party begins recognizing:

- Instability patterns
- Signs of recent Dungeon changes
- Adaptive or evolving resonance behavior
- Irregularities within the archway’s structure
- Clues regarding how the Dungeon responds to intrusion

At this level, experienced delvers may begin suspecting the presence of concealed or suppressed resonance influences.

## Hidden Understanding (10 VP)
The party uncovers truths hidden beneath the visible rune structure.

This may reveal:
- Hidden Attunements
- Concealed Warning Runes
- Signs of Awakening or Seed agitation
- Evidence of intelligent or reactive Dungeon behavior
- Suppressed resonance patterns
- Dangerous metaphysical anomalies

Reaching this level of understanding is rare and often dangerous, as prolonged exposure to the archway’s resonance may draw the Dungeon’s attention.

## Relevant Skills

### Arcana
Interpret magical resonance, essence interaction, and arcane rune structures.

### Occultism
Discern concealed resonance patterns, hidden meanings, and metaphysical distortions.

### Religion
Interpret divine symbolism, cosmological structure, and ancient sacred rune patterns.

### Nature
Understand ecological expression, biological resonance, and environmental behavior reflected within the archway.

Additional skills such as Perception, Society, Crafting, or Lore skills may provide secondary benefits or contextual insight depending on the Dungeon.
